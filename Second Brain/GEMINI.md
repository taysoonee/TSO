# Purpose

This wiki serves as the Market Intelligence Second Brain for Taylor's Schools (K-12). Key domains include:

- Competitor tracking (Fees, expansions, promotions).
- Daily funnel and enrolment data synthesis.
- Macro-demographic and economic research.
- Feasibility and survey analysis.

# Folder Structure

- `/raw`: Read-only source documents (CSV exports, news clippings). Do not modify.
- `/wiki`: Generated markdown files, competitor profiles, and concept nodes.
- `/Reports`: Departmental daily/weekly syntheses.
- `/Projects`: Ad-hoc deep dives and feasibility studies.

# The Ingest Workflow

1. **No Chronological Stacks:** Integrate new facts seamlessly into the main body text of the competitor or market node.
2. **Mandatory Linkage:** Every wiki page must contain `[[wikilinks]]` connecting it to other nodes (e.g., `[[Competitor X]]` linked to `[[Thesis 2: M40 Squeeze]]`).
3. **The Contradiction Rule:** If a competitor changes their fee structure, do not delete the old data. Create a `## Pricing Shift` sub-heading.

# Intelligence Monitoring & Triggers

1. **Competitor Expansion (Mandatory Alert):** If a top-tier competitor announces a new campus, land acquisition, or major capacity upgrade, immediately flag as `[EXECUTIVE ALERT]`.
2. **Fee & Bursary Warfare:** Monitor for aggressive off-cycle discounts or scholarship drives by direct competitors. Route immediately to `[MARKETING]`.
3. **MOE Policy Shifts:** Any news regarding the Ministry of Education, curriculum mandates, or private school licensing must be isolated and flagged for strategic review.
