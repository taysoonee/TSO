# International School @ ParkCity (ISP)

## Catchment Growth &amp; Capacity Velocity
ISP is the prevailing benchmark for hyper-rapid geographic monopolisation within closed-loop, affluent master-planned residential townships. This perfectly operationalises `[[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]]`. Initiating operations in mid-2012 with a baseline intake of 135 students, the facility quickly accelerated past 200 enrolled students by its June opening. 

This momentum was completely sustained; by the 2014-2015 academic year, total enrolment had violently surged past 750 physical students spanning 48 nationalities. ISP is rapidly accelerating towards its final master-planned saturation target of 1,200 physical seats.

## Hardware &amp; Academic Value Proposition
Unlike older institutional rivals, ISP executed a technologically dependent academic strategy integrated into the standard UK curriculum. The mandate enforcing 1-to-1 iPads from Year 3 onwards, paired with a blanket wi-fi enabled compound, positioned them as an early-adopting modern academy. By capturing the domestic wealth trapped within the prime Desa ParkCity catchment, the school maintains immunity against the lower-tier impact of the `[[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]]`.
