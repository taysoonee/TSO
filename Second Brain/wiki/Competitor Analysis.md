## Key Metrics Tracked
Competitor analysis relies on extracting public and proprietary financial data to benchmark:
- **Net Revenue & Growth**
- **Direct Staff Costs & Ratios:** Monitoring how much competitors spend on academic staff relative to revenue (a key factor in the group's own staff localization strategies).
- **EBITDA & Net Profit:** Understanding the financial health and sustainability of competing business models.

### Update (May 13, 2026): Historical Enrolment Benchmarking (ISKL vs MKIS)
- **Macro Sensitivity:** Historical enrolment data (2014-2020) for top-tier competitors ISKL and MKIS confirms high sensitivity to macro-expatriate shocks, specifically the 2014/2015 Oil & Gas price collapse. This validates [[The Living Ledger]] [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate & FDI Inflow Vector]].
- **ISKL Recovery via Infrastructure:** ISKL's enrolment declined from 1,747 (2014) to a low of 1,457 (2017) during the O&G downturn. However, it rebounded strongly to 1,623 (2018) and 1,700 (2019), correlating directly with the opening of their new campus. This proves the ROI of major greenfield/brownfield investments in recovering market share (validating [[The Living Ledger]] [[Brownfield Saturation Limits]]).
- **MKIS Attrition:** Without a similar catalyst, Mont Kiara International School (MKIS) suffered significant attrition following the O&G crash, dropping from 804 (2015) to 625 (2016). It showed a long-term downward trend, reaching 571 by 2019 before stabilizing at 600 in 2020.
