# Project ISKL Campus Bid

In September 2018, Taylor's Schools (via Educrest Sdn. Bhd.) proposed bidding for the former International School of Kuala Lumpur (ISKL) campuses following their relocation to Ampang Hilir. The goal was to lease the acquired space to **Nexus International School Malaysia (NISM)** for expansion.

## Campus Comparison

| Feature | Melawati Campus | Ampang Campus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Land Size** | 3.7 + 3.8 acres (~326,028 sqft) | 6.4 acres (~278,784 sqft) |
| **Tenure** | Freehold | Leasehold (exp. June 2088) |
| **Capacity** | ~700 students | ~1,200 students |
| **Indicative Price** | RM70 million | RM90 million |
| **Feasibility** | **Not Feasible** | **Feasible (Complex)** |

## Strategic Rationale
- **Melawati Site:** Deemed not feasible due to small capacity (harder to breakeven) and original design being suited for primary school use.
- **Ampang Site:** Feasible due to strategic location (within 10km of city centre) and appropriate size. However, several complications were identified:
    - DBKL road reserve in front of school (no rental).
    - Land behind school leased from community centre (~RM40k/year).
    - Proximity to a cemetery.
    - Requirement to tear down and rebuild existing structures.
    - Potential cannibalization risk with TISKL (within 2km).

## Key Timeline (EOI Process)
- **13 Sept 2018:** NDA signed and site visit conducted by [[Investment Committee]] members.
- **28 Sept 2018:** Deadline for submission of non-binding EOI to Savills Malaysia.
- **Nov 2018:** Submission of final binding offers by selected parties.
- **Dec 2018:** Signing of SPA and 10% downpayment.
- **April 2019:** Estimated sale completion.
