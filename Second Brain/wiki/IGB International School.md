# IGB International School
**Jurisdiction:** Malaysia
**Corporate Entity:** Detik Harapan Sdn. Bhd. (Holding company: IGB Corporation Berhad)

## Financial & Operational Baselines

### 2014
- **Revenue:** RM 4,071,601 (RM 3.8M from school fees).
- **Profit:** Loss of RM 15,228,945.

### 2015
- **Revenue:** RM 14,227,304 (RM 14M from school fees).
- **Profit:** Loss of RM 22,955,932.

### 2016
- **Revenue:** RM 19,668,832 (RM 19.2M from school fees).
- **Profit:** Loss of RM 25,206,224.
- **Strategic Notes:** The institution operates at a severe and persistent deficit, carrying net current liabilities of RM 175.9M and a total equity deficit of RM 71M as of 2016. However, it relies heavily on continuous financial life support from IGB Corporation Berhad. This high-CAPEX, long-burn strategy is highly vulnerable to the economic limits outlined in [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]].
