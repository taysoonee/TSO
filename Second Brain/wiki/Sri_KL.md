# Sri KL (Othman Merican Educational Development Berhad)

## Financial Architecture & Tax Immunity
Sri KL, incorporated as Othman Merican Educational Development Berhad (a company limited by guarantee), operates an exceptionally capital-efficient, tax-exempt model under the Income Tax (Exemption) (No. 5) Order 2008. Commencing full operations post-2010 after acquiring RM 24 million in business assets via an extended property lease, their trajectory has been explosive. By 2016, revenues cleared RM 47.5 million with net profit breaching RM 8.7 million. 

## Strategic Threat Level
By circumventing standard corporate taxation, Sri KL commands an artificial OPEX advantage over standard for-profit international networks. This enables them to maintain immensely competitive tuition levels, transforming them into a prime defensive recipient for standard-tier candidates fleeing premium tiers due to [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]]. Their 260-strong employee base and robust, high-margin fee strata (which actively monetises peripheral funnels like exams and entrance testing) position them as a dominant force in the domestic transit corridor.
