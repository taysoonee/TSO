# Project Tanjung 12

A potential new school site proposed by [[Gamuda Berhad]].

## Strategic Analysis

- **Recommendation:** To keep interest warm for long-term strategy.
- **Rationale:** The location overlaps with the existing markets of [[Taylor's International School Puchong]] and [[Nexus International School Malaysia]]. Gamuda has multiple land pieces in the Klang Valley, which may present future opportunities.
