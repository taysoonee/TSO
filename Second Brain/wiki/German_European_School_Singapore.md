# German European School Singapore (GESS)

German European School Singapore (GESS) is a competitor in the Singapore international school market, offering distinct German and European (IB) curricula.

## Strategic Positioning
GESS caters to diverse expatriate needs by providing multiple curricula and offering flexible short-term enrolment options (up to 6 months). This flexibility is a key differentiator for attracting transient families, aligning with [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate &amp; FDI Inflow Vector]].

## Fee Structure Analysis
- **2015-2016:** Annual tuition fees for the European Section ranged from S\$24,310 (Primary) to S\$31,930 (IB DP). The school charged a one-time entrance fee of S\$2,945 and a tiered Development Levy.
