# Kolej Tuanku Ja'afar (KTJ)

Kolej Tuanku Ja'afar (KTJ) is a Tier-1 competitor in the Malaysian private education market, with a strong focus on residential (boarding) school services.

## Financial Performance &amp; Strategy

### FY2018
- **Revenue:** RM 58,030,954
- **Profit:** RM 6,130,772
- **Strategic Note:** KTJ's primary activity as a residential school directly aligns with the mechanism of [[Thesis 3: Boarding as a Premium Utility]]. The school is exempted from income tax, which allows for a potentially more competitive fee structure or greater reinvestment into facilities. It had substantial capital commitments of RM 16.1 million for property and equipment, indicating significant investment in its infrastructure to support its premium boarding offering. Its revenue management policy, requiring a deposit of one term's fees, helps manage cash flow and mitigate credit risk, a prudent measure in a market affected by [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]].
