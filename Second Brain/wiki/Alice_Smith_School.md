# The Alice Smith Schools Association

## Corporate Structure & Capital Acquisition
Operating as a 'Limited by Guarantee' entity, Alice Smith utilises a highly robust non-profit model. It possesses a tax-exempt status for educational business income. Crucially, facility development is bankrolled by non-refundable Building Fund Contributions and, strategically, via unsecured, interest-free 'loans' from parents (RM 3.36M in short-term borrowings in 2016), effectively converting their premium parent base into a zero-cost liquidity pool.

## Expansion & Liquidity
The Association commands a formidable liquidity position (holding RM 79.1M in short-term investments as of 2016). It maintains an active mandate for land acquisition, committing RM 5.3M for a 1,022 sqm parcel in Kuala Lumpur. This aggressive land banking in prime catchment zones threatens our future footprint viability and accelerates site scarcity under [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]]. The school generated a healthy operational surplus of RM 4.06M in 2016 on net fees of RM 81.3M, demonstrating premium fee elasticity.
