# The British School of Kuala Lumpur (BSKL)

The British School of Kuala Lumpur (BSKL) is a premium Tier-1 competitor in the Malaysian international school market. It is part of the global Nord Anglia Education group.

## Financial Performance

### FY2019 (16-month period)
- **Revenue:** RM 120,770,360
- **Profit:** RM 4,838,948
- **Strategic Note:** The school is ultimately held by Baring Private Equity Asia, indicating strong private equity backing. A notable operational detail is that certain rentals for campus and staff accommodation are tied to a percentage of tuition fees, creating a variable cost structure that scales with revenue.
