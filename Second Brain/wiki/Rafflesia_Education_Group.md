# Rafflesia Education Group

Rafflesia Education Group Sdn Bhd is a competitor in the Malaysian private school market, operating Rafflesia International School campuses in Puchong and Kajang.

## Financial Performance &amp; Strategy

### FY2015
- **Revenue:** RM 16,188,477
- **Profit/(Loss):** RM (3,125,689)
- **Strategic Note:** Despite reporting a net loss, the group had a substantial capital commitment of RM 38 million authorised for the purchase of leasehold land in Sepang. This indicates a strong focus on future expansion and new campus development, a key strategic move relevant to [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]].

### FY2014
- **Revenue:** RM 8,758,920
- **Profit/(Loss):** RM (6,946,969)
- **Strategic Note:** The group recorded a substantial net loss in FY2014. However, it had a large balance of 'Fees received in advance' (RM 11.18 million), suggesting strong forward enrolments or prepayment structures that support cash flow despite operational losses.
