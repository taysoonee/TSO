# Brighton College (Singapore)

Brighton College (Singapore) is a premium international school competitor in the Singapore market.

## Fee Structure &amp; Strategy
The school's fee schedule positions it firmly in the premium segment, with substantial non-refundable upfront fees to secure commitment. It employs sibling discounts to attract and retain multi-child families.

- **2023-2024:** Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$16,215 (Pre-Nursery Half Day) to S\$43,839 (Years 7-8). The school noted the impending GST increase from 8% to 9% in January 2024. Sibling discounts of 5-10% were offered.
[ARCHIVE: Brighton 2023.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/Brighton College (Singapore)/2023]

- **2022-2023:** Annual tuition for Years 3-6 was S\$38,130. The fee schedule reflected the GST increase from 7% to 8% effective 1 January 2023.
[ARCHIVE: Brighton 2022.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/Brighton College (Singapore)/2022]

- **2021-2022:** Annual tuition for Years 3-6 was S\$36,350. Sibling discounts of 5-10% were available. Total upfront fees (Application, Enrolment, Development) amounted to S\$7,500.
