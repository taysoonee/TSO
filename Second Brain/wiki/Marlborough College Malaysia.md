## 2013 Prospectus & Institutional Setup
- **Campus:** 90-acre secure estate in Iskandar Malaysia (Nusajaya).
- **Structure:**
    - Pre-Preparatory (Ages 4-7): Reception to Year 2 (Day only).
    - Preparatory (Ages 7-13): Year 3 to Year 8 (Boarding option from Year 5/Age 9).
    - Senior School (Ages 13-18): Year 9 to Year 13 (Boarding and Day).
- **Curriculum:**
    - EYFS / British National Curriculum / Common Entrance.
    - IGCSE (Years 10-11).
    - IB Diploma Programme (Years 12-13).
- **Leadership Strategy:** Master Robert B Pick (former Deputy Head of MCM UK) leads the school. The school emphasizes 'beak' (teacher) and pupil exchanges with the UK Wiltshire campus, positioning itself as a 'genuine extension' rather than a franchise.
- **Logistics:** Day pupil transport provided for Singapore and Johor Bahru catchment areas.
- **Strategic Alignment:** Directly supports [[Thesis 3: Boarding as a Premium Utility]] and [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate & FDI Inflow Vector]] by leveraging the Iskandar-Singapore corridor.
- **Source:** [[2013 Marlborough College Malaysia Prospectus.pdf]]
