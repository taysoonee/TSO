# Chatsworth International School

Chatsworth International School Pte Ltd is a competitor in the Singapore international school market, positioning itself as an 'Affordable International School' offering 'exceptional value'.

## Financial Performance

### FY2017 (Year ended 31 August 2017)
- **Revenue:** S\$24,863,335
- **Profit:** S\$2,576,096
- **Strategic Note:** The school's principal activities focus on providing education for expatriate students, directly aligning its target market with [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate &amp; FDI Inflow Vector]]. It operates on a capital-light model with significant operating lease commitments for its premises.

## Fee Structure &amp; Strategy
Chatsworth's fee strategy supports its 'affordable value' positioning. It offers sibling discounts and has used promotional waivers to drive enrolment.

- **2022-2023:** Annual tuition fees (with 8% GST) ranged from S\$25,298 (Kindergarten) to S\$34,753 (Years 12-13). It offered a 25% sibling discount for the third child onwards.
[ARCHIVE: Chats - 2022.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/Chatsworth International School/2022]

- **2020-2021:** Annual tuition fees (with 7% GST) ranged from S\$23,819 to S\$32,788.
[ARCHIVE: Chats - 2020.pdf -&gt; 2 Singapore Schools/Chatsworth International School/2020]

- **2019-2020:** The school ran a promotional offer for its Bukit Timah campus, waiving the application fee and offering 20% off the confirmation fee. This is a clear example of promotional tactics to drive enrolment in a specific catchment area.
