# The Perse School Singapore

The Perse School Singapore is a competitor in the Singapore international school market.

## Market Entry Strategy
The school employed a highly aggressive market entry strategy, using significant discounts and fee waivers to build enrolment in its initial years. This is a clear example of 'Fee &amp; Bursary Warfare'.

- **2020-2021:** Offered a 30% discount on standard tuition fees (S\$25,000 reduced to S\$17,500) and waived all one-time fees (Application, Enrolment, Assessment).
- **2021-2022:** Offered a 15% discount on tuition fees (S\$25,000 reduced to S\$21,250).
