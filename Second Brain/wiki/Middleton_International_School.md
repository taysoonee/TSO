# Middleton International School

Middleton International School is a competitor in the Singapore market, affiliated with the EtonHouse group. It operates multiple campuses, including Tampines and Upper Bukit Timah.

## Strategic Positioning &amp; Expansion
The school is undergoing phased expansion, with plans to progressively launch higher grades (G10 onwards from 2022). This expansion is a key competitive move to monitor under [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]]. It implements a 'Bring Your Own Device' (BYOD) policy from Grade 3/4.

## Fee Structure Analysis
The school's fee structure appears to target a mid-to-high tier segment of the market.

- **2021 (Tampines):** Total annual course fees ranged from S\$17,173 (Early Years) to S\$18,731 (Secondary). The school offered an 'AY2021 Fees Discount', indicating promotional activity.
