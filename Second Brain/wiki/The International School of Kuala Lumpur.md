# The International School of Kuala Lumpur (ISKL)
**Jurisdiction:** [[Malaysia]]

## Financial & Operational Performance
During the 2011-2012 financial period, ISKL recorded a substantial operational surplus of RM16.2 million. The institution comfortably accommodated a student body of 1,706 pupils. 

## Infrastructure & Expansion Strategy
In alignment with demographic shifts outlined in [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]] and broader [[Competitor Analysis]], the school embarked on an aggressive infrastructure scalability strategy. Strategic operations actively sought a new 25-acre campus site to bypass growth ceilings at current facilities. To securely finance this large-scale capital expenditure, RM13 million was strategically transferred into their established Land and Building Reserve, augmenting the dedicated fund to a total of RM83 million. The period also featured extensive structural and operational preparation for a transition to a new Head of School, slated for July 2013.
