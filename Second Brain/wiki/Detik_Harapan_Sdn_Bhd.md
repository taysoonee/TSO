# Detik Harapan Sdn. Bhd.

Detik Harapan Sdn. Bhd. operates as an educational institution in Malaysia.

## Financial Performance

### FY2017
- **Revenue:** RM 21,125,978
- **Profit/(Loss):** RM (23,929,571)
- **Strategic Note:** The company recorded significant net current liabilities of RM 195.7 million and a total equity deficit of RM 94.9 million. Its ability to continue as a 'going concern' was entirely dependent on confirmed financial support from its immediate holding company, IGB Corporation Berhad. This severe financial distress highlights significant operational risk and reliance on its parent entity for survival.
