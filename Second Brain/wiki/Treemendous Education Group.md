# Treemendous Education Group (TEG)

Treemendous Education Group is an early years education provider operating in Malaysia, with a focus on Montessori-based curricula integrated with local language and primary school preparation programs.

## Campus Locations
- **Tadika Eko Ceria (Eco Ardence, Setia Alam):** Located at Lot 32, PT8, Persiaran Setia Alam, Eco Ardence, 40170 Shah Alam, Selangor.

## Operational Model (as of 2022-2023)
- **School Hours:** Session begins at 8:30 AM; earliest drop-off at 7:50 AM.
- **Dismissal Times (Half Day):**
    - 4yo: 12:00 PM
    - 5yo: 12:30 PM
    - 6yo: 12:15 PM
- **Full Day / Daycare:** Available with dismissal anytime before 6:30 PM. Includes afternoon nap and milk/meal provisions.
- **Logistics:** Utilizes the Jaya Grocer roundabout (Eco Ardence) as a primary drop-off and transporter pick-up point.
- **Digital Infrastructure:** Uses **DingTalk** for parent communication, health declarations, and vaccination tracking.

## Curriculum & Pedagogy
The group employs a blended Montessori approach:
- **Core:** Montessori Practical Life, Sensorial, Phonics, Mathematics, and Cultural.
- **Language Integration:** English, Mandarin, and Bahasa Malaysia.
- **Academic Preparation:** The 6-year-old 'Mighty Tree' program focuses on 'Primary Mathematics Preparation' (conducted in Mandarin) and 'Saya Sedia BM Tahun 1', specifically targeting the feeder market for vernacular (SJKC) and international schools.
- **Experiential Learning:** Incorporates community-based learning, such as 'Real life shopping experience' visits to Jaya Grocer.

## Strategic Significance
TEG acts as a high-quality early years competitor in premium residential corridors like Setia Alam. Their focus on rigorous primary school readiness and convenient operational features (e.g., Jaya Grocer pick-up, full-day options) makes them a key institution to monitor for early years lead generation and as a potential feeder for [[Taylor's International School]] or [[Garden International School]].

## Intelligence Update (May 14, 2026) - K1 Academic Rigor (Spelling)
- **Trilingual Academic Push (Term 2 2023):** TEG implements a structured trilingual spelling (Ejaan/Dictation) program at the Kindergarten 1 (4-5 year old) level.
    - **English:** Progresses from simple CVC words (am, ig, ot, um) to blending (brim, frog, drum, grin) by Week 12.
    - **Bahasa Melayu:** Focuses on vowel recognition (a, e, i, o, u) and basic syllable formation (bi, ba, bu, ca, ci, cu) before moving to simple words like 'cari' and 'baju'.
    - **Mandarin:** Focuses on numbers (1-10) and basic spatial/relational characters (up, down, big, small, male, female, mouth, head, hand, tooth, ear).
- **Strategic Impact:** This confirms TEG's positioning as an 'Academic Readiness' hub. By introducing formal spelling tests (held on Wednesdays-Fridays depending on class group) at K1, they cater to domestic parents (M40/T20) who prioritize measurable academic performance. This academic 'anchoring' makes it harder for families to transition to more play-based international school early years, validating [[Brownfield Saturation Limits]] regarding competitor depth in the Setia Alam catchment.
