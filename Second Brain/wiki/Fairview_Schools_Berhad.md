# Fairview Schools Berhad

Fairview Schools Berhad operates a group of English-medium private international schools across Malaysia and the UK, following the British education syllabus. It is a significant competitor with a multi-campus strategy.

## Financial Performance &amp; Strategy

### FY2019 (Year ended 30 June 2019)
- **Revenue:** RM 31,118,138
- **Profit:** RM 5,937,024
- **Strategic Note:** The audit report issued a qualified opinion because the company did not prepare consolidated financial statements for its subsidiary, hindering a complete view of the group's financial health. Total borrowings increased significantly to RM 42.1 million, suggesting active financing for operations or expansion. A slight decrease in school fee revenue may indicate market pressures linked to [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]] or increased competition.

### FY2015 (Year ended 31 July 2015)
- **Revenue:** RM 30,689,201
