# Market Intelligence Operations

## Intelligence Directives
The Market Intelligence (MI) element within Taylor's Schools operates to systematically audit competitor positioning, structural expansion, and macroeconomic vulnerabilities. This intelligence directly informs the strategic alignment of the K-12 portfolio, influencing M&A feasibility and operational hedging metrics.

## Surveillance Vectors & Methodologies

### Corporate Auditing
To monitor market saturation dynamics and track competitor capital mobility, the MI function executes rigorous corporate intelligence cycles:
- **Financial Compliance Monitoring:** Systematic review of competitor corporate filings and financial health. Operational intensity escalated significantly from baseline monitoring in 2021 to high-frequency surveillance loops between October 2022 and April 2023, correlating with structural realignments across the education sector.
- **Geospatial & Entity Surveillance:** Integration of land title and entity tracking via legal intelligence registries (commencing Q2 2023). This capability supports predictive modelling of competitor greenfield expansions and validates regional catchment viability under [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]].

### Field Operations & Hyper-Local Entrenchment
- **Domestic Corridors:** Continuous strategic placement of intelligence personnel within core domestic growth intersections, notably the Setia Alam / Bukit Raja axis, secures real-time interception of hyper-local data. This facilitates advanced tracking of competitor density paradigms (e.g., [[Treemendous Education Group]]).
- **Regional Deployment:** Intelligence exercises encompass direct surveillance of supplementary markets (e.g., the Penang market geometry supporting [[Project TIS Penang]]) alongside assessments of North Asian expatriate mobility corridors feeding into [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate & FDI Inflow Vector]].

## Funnel Digitisation Integration
Digitisation of corporate lead-capture infrastructure supplements passive intelligence mechanisms. By integrating real-time demographic analytics at the point of origin, the framework synchronises overarching market surveillance with immediate operational conversion outcomes.
