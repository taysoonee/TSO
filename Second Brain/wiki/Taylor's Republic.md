# Taylor's Republic

## Overview
Taylor's Republic was a strategic initiative (circa 2017) focused on redefining operational frameworks across Taylor's Schools. It utilized a 'Pemandu Lab' methodology—a structured brainstorming and prioritization process—to set up taskforces for implementing institutional blueprints.

## Key Work Streams
- **Operations Optimization:** Focused on efficiency gains, such as the [[Australian International School Malaysia]], [[Garden International School]], and [[Nexus International School Malaysia]] bookstore outsourcing project.
- **Blueprint Implementation:** Collaborative labs involving senior leadership to create clear definitions and implementation paths for the Taylor's Schools vision.

## Historical Stakeholders
- **Project Lead:** [[Tay Soon Ee]]
- **Advisory/Facilitation:** Audrey, Andy Low, Thou Kok Cheng, Mushtak, and Rita (Pemandu).

## Talent Pipeline Synergy (Historical KPI)
- **University to K-12 Pipeline:** An early strategic priority (circa 2014-2015) set a target for 25% of teachers for [[Taylor's International School]] to be recruited directly from [[Taylor's University]] (Education faculty) graduates, cementing the internal talent ecosystem model.
