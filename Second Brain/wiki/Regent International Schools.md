# Regent International Schools

Regent International Schools operates in the Malaysian K-12 market.

## Strategic Movements & Market Positioning

### Campus Expansion & Consolidation (2021)
- **Bukit Jalil Relocation:** In December 2021, Regent relocated its Puchong campus to a new facility in Technology Park Malaysia, Bukit Jalil (taking over the former APIIS campus). This campus is structured to cater exclusively to secondary students (Year 7 to Year 11). This consolidation reflects a strategic focus on secondary education within the highly competitive Klang Valley corridor, impacting local capacity limits (linked to [[The Living Ledger]] Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits and [[Competitor Analysis]]).
- **Regional Expansion (Perak):** Regent expanded its portfolio outside the Klang Valley with the establishment of 'Regent De La Salle International School' in Taiping, Perak. The new campus offers Key Stage 3 and 4 (ages 11-16) Cambridge IGCSE programs. It leverages a unique positioning by being situated on historic church grounds next to SMK Convent Taiping, targeting the regional secondary market.

### Brand Positioning via Sports Sponsorship
- **BAM Partnership:** Regent has engaged in strategic brand positioning by sponsoring the education of junior players from the Badminton Association of Malaysia (BAM). Regent teachers provide home-schooling and IGCSE tutoring for these national athletes. This acts as a high-visibility CSR and PR move to align the school's brand with national sports excellence.
