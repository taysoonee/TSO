Taylor's Education Group utilizes a standardized grading system for its employees across various divisions.

- **S13/S14/S15**
- **T16/T17**

## Professional and Individual Contributors (P/IC)
- **P5/P6/P7**
- **IC10/IC11/IC12/IC13**

## Managerial and Executive (M)
- **M10/M11/M12**

## New Job Grading & Salary Structure (Division Level)
- **Implementation:** Evaluation of 127 job positions for 184 staff across the School Division.
- **Benchmarking:** Aligned to market median using data from **Towers Watson**, **Mercer**, and **Hays** (covering education and general industries).
- **Emplacement Outcome:**
    - 79.3% (146 staff) within range.
    - 13.1% (24 staff) below minimum; adjusted to floor.
    - 7.6% (14 staff) above ceiling; increments frozen until promotion.
- **Career Pathways Model:** Introduced dual pathways:
    1. **Management Pathway:** For those overseeing people and organizational strategy.
    2. **Specialist/Technical Pathway:** For experts and individual contributors (e.g., IT Specialists, Business Analysts, Psychologists) focused on scientific or practical expertise.

## Grade-Specific Entitlements (2012 Historical)
Historical travel and allowance entitlements based on job grades (per [[Summary of Claims.pdf]]):
- **Top Management (T16-T17, S13-S15):** Business Class travel, 5-star accommodation, actual expense reimbursement or lump sum subsistence (RM60).
- **Senior Management (S13-S14):** Economy Class travel, 3-star accommodation.
- **Academic & Support (P5-P9, M10-M12):** RM180-200 accommodation room charges; RM60 subsistence.
- **Non-Executive (NC, NE1-NE4):** RM120-130 accommodation room charges; RM35 subsistence.
