# Dulwich College Singapore (DCS)

Dulwich College Singapore (DCS) is a premium Tier-1 international school operating in the Singapore K-12 market. It serves as a benchmark for high-end expatriate pricing and marketing retention strategies.

## Pricing Shift: 2020/21 Annual Tuition Fees

As of the 2020/21 academic year, DCS maintained a premium fee structure with significant upfront entry costs, positioning it at the top of the market alongside SAS and SAIS.

- **One-off Entry Costs:** 
  - Application Fee (Non-refundable): SGD 4,500 (approx. RM 13,905)
  - Capital Levy: SGD 3,750 (approx. RM 11,587)
  - **Total Initial Friction:** SGD 8,250 (approx. RM 25,492)
  
- **Annual Tuition Fees (Key Years):**
  - Toddler / Nursery (full day): SGD 30,060 (approx. RM 92,885)
  - Reception - Year 2: SGD 36,240 (approx. RM 111,981)
  - Years 7 - 9: SGD 43,140 (approx. RM 133,302)
  - Years 10 - 11: SGD 45,300 (approx. RM 139,977)
  - Years 12 - 13: SGD 46,840 (approx. RM 144,735)

*(Note: MYR conversions based on an exchange rate of approximately 3.09)*

### Retention & Marketing Tactics
To maintain enrolment stability amidst high fees, DCS deploys strategic discounts:
1. **Volume Discounting:** Families with 3 or more children enrolled receive a 5% discount on tuition fees for students in Reception and above.
2. **Loyalty Lock-in:** A one-time loyalty discount of 15% on Term 3 (2019/2020) tuition was credited to eligible students in Term 1 (2020/2021). This functions as a direct retention incentive to prevent holiday-period attrition.

**Strategic Impact:** The massive entry costs and premium tuition at DCS represent a high barrier to entry in the Singapore market. This provides an arbitrage opportunity for Taylor's group schools to competitively market their Malaysian premium and boarding options to cost-sensitive expatriates, directly supporting [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate & FDI Inflow Vector]].
