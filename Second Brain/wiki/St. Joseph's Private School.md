# St. Joseph's Private School (SJPS)

St. Joseph's Private School is a major competitor in the Kuching, Sarawak region.

## Fees (Effective September 2012)
- **Primary (Tahun 1-3):** RM 3,600 per year.
- **Secondary (Tingkatan 5):** RM 6,200 per year.
