# Dover Court International School

Dover Court International School (Pte.) Ltd. is a premium competitor in the Singapore market, operating as part of the global Nord Anglia Education group.

## Strategic Positioning
The school differentiates itself by offering specialised and inclusive education programmes, including English as an Additional Language (EAL) support and a dedicated Department for Supportive Education (DSE) for students with specific learning difficulties ('Pathways' programmes). This broadens its appeal to a wider range of expatriate families with diverse needs.

## Financial Performance

### FY2016 (Year ended 31 August 2016)
- **Revenue:** S\$24,879,814
- **Profit:** S\$5,445,653
- **Strategic Note:** The company reported strong profitability. It operates a Building Fund, where students contribute towards capital expenditures, indicating a sustainable model for facility investment.

## Fee Structure Analysis
Dover Court's fees are in the premium tier, with specific pricing for its mainstream and supportive education pathways.

- **2021-2022:** Annual mainstream tuition ranged from S\$22,470 (Nursery) to S\$34,876 (Year 13). Its specialised 'Pathway' programmes for supportive education were significantly higher, reaching S\$45,210.
[ARCHIVE: Dover - 2021.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/Dover Court International School/2021]
