# Stamford American International School Pte. Ltd. (Cognita)

## Conglomerate Supremacy in Singapore
Backed by Cognita Holdings Limited, Stamford American represents the zenith of premium expatriate harvesting, confirming the high ceiling modelled in [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate & FDI Inflow Vector]]. During FY 2017, the single entity produced a staggering S$ 109.9 million in revenue—primarily sustained by tuition—securing S$ 9.2 million in pre-tax net.

## Inter-Company Financing Model
The institution avoids traditional external banking reliance, instead leveraging immense inter-company loan pipelines from its parent network. This results in heavy related-party debt structures and calculated interest load distributions. A crucial component of their liquidity is a mandatory upfront facility fee, which their actuaries strategically amortise over a projected average student tenure limit of 32 months.
