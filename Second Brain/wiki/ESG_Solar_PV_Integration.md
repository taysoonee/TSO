# ESG: Solar PV Integration Project

The Solar PV Integration project is a group-wide capital expenditure initiative to install solar photovoltaic systems across key Taylor's Schools campuses. This project serves as a strategic operational hedge against utility inflation and rising operating expenditure (OPEX).

## Strategic Rationale
This initiative directly supports [[The Living Ledger]] [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]]. By fixing a significant portion of energy costs, the schools mitigate the risk of OPEX-driven fee hikes. This helps maintain affordability and competitiveness, particularly for the price-sensitive domestic T20/M40 segments, without compromising EBITDA margins. The project also aligns with broader corporate ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) goals.
