# Overseas Family School (OFS)

## Demographic Contraction (2018)
OFS acts as a critical canary in the coalmine regarding systemic fluctuations in Singaporean visa flows. Their FY 2018 statements formally acknowledged "weaker student enrolments," providing concrete, audited validation of underlying macroeconomic threats to [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate & FDI Inflow Vector]].

## Balance Sheet Defences
Despite the dip in raw acquisition, their scale provides a formidable safety net. Total FY 2018 revenues held high at S$ 83 million (generating S$ 6.9 million net). To protect operating liquidity during the enrolment slump, management shifted to conservative internal expenditure models and engineered a strategic debt restructuring, reclassifying legacy bonds into current liabilities pending integration into long-term commercial bank facilities.
