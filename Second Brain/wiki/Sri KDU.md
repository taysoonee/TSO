# Sri KDU
**Jurisdiction:** Malaysia
**Corporate Entity:** Sri KDU Sdn. Bhd. (Formerly KDU Smart School Sdn. Bhd. until April 2017. Holding company: Paramount Corporation Berhad).

## Financial & Operational Baselines

### 2014
- **Revenue:** RM 72,975,164.
- **Profit:** RM 24,984,489.

### 2015
- **Revenue:** RM 81,694,619.
- **Profit:** RM 26,310,965.
- **Strategic Notes:** Maintained significant non-cancellable operating lease commitments for campus facilities.

### 2017
- **Revenue:** RM 86,527,630.
- **Profit:** RM 80,491,225 (Profit net of tax).
- **Strategic Notes:** The dramatic 2017 profit jump was highly anomalistic, driven by 'other income' amounting to RM 62.9M rather than core tuition growth. This occurred concurrently with the corporate name change to Sri KDU Sdn. Bhd. The entity also facilitated significant cash outflows related to dividend payments back to Paramount Corp.
