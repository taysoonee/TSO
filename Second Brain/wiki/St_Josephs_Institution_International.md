# St. Joseph's Institution International (SJI)

St. Joseph's Institution International is a premium competitor in the Singapore market, operating as a school of the De La Salle Brothers. It operates separate Elementary and High School sections.

## Fee Structure &amp; Strategy
SJI's fee structure is in the premium tier and includes specific Development Fees for campus upkeep. It offers a significant sibling discount to attract and retain large families.

### High School
- **2023:** Total annual fees ranged from S\$35,038 (G7-8) to S\$44,192 (G11). A 25% sibling discount was offered for the fourth child onwards. Fees included an 8% GST.
[ARCHIVE: SJI Sec - 2023.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/St. Joseph's Institution International/2023]

- **2022:** Total annual fees ranged from S\$33,059 to S\$41,671.
[ARCHIVE: SJI 2022.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/St. Joseph's Institution International/2022]

- **2021:** The school kept fees at 'status quo' for 2021. Annual tuition ranged from S\$29,168 to S\$34,058.
[ARCHIVE: SJI 2021.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/St. Joseph's Institution International/2021]

- **2014:** Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$23,276 to S\$27,248.
[ARCHIVE: SJI.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/St Joseph's Institution International/2014]

### Elementary School
- **2023:** Annual school fees were approximately S\$35,000. The fee structure reflected the GST increase from 7% to 8% mid-year.
