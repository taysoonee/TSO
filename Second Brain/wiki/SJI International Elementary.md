# St Joseph’s Institution International Elementary School (SJIIES)
**Jurisdiction:** Singapore

## Financial & Operational Baselines

### FY 2017
- **Revenue:** S$ 20,412,000 (Fees S$ 20.2M).
- **Profit:** S$ 3,990,000.
- **Strategic Notes:** The school strictly isolates and reserves its operational surplus for future lease renewal and new campus development targeted for 2022. It manages immense central overhead, paying a 39% share operating cost up to the SJII Central Administration block.
