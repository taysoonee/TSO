# Project TISPJ

Project TISPJ was a strategic evaluation (circa 2021) focused on expanding the [[Taylor's International School]] brand into the Petaling Jaya catchment through the acquisition of [[Sri Bestari International School]].

## Strategic Rationale
- **Market Entry:** Targeted the high-growth PJ corridor with a 'Value-Cambridge' offering.
- **Brand Synergy:** Plan to rebrand the existing institution to **TISPJ** to leverage Taylor's brand equity.
- **Capacity Expansion:** Proposed development to reach a capacity of **2,450 students** across primary and secondary streams.

## Financial & Operational Intelligence (2020)
- **Target Entity:** Lang Education Sdn Bhd (wholly-owned by [[Land & General Berhad]]).
- **Revenue:** RM 19,559,023.
- **EBITDA:** RM 5,891,350 (30% margin).
- **Enrolment:** 1,400 students (57% capacity) across local and international curricula.
- **Asset Valuation:** Buildings Net Book Value (NBV) at ~RM 41.5M.
- **Key Deadline:** Put option to purchase land/buildings by **31 December 2024**.

## Proposed Roadmap (2021-2026)
- **2021:** Conclude transaction and review school structure.
- **2022:** Phase out local curriculum and plan campus refurbishment.
- **2023:** Rebrand to **TIS** and begin 3-year refurbishment plan.
- **2024:** Launch **TISPJ**.
