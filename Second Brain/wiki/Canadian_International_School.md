# Canadian International School (CIS)

Canadian International School (CIS) Pte Ltd is a major competitor in the Singapore premium international school market, with two campuses (Lakeside and Tanjong Katong).

## Strategic Positioning
CIS differentiates itself by offering diverse language streams, including Chinese-English and French-English bilingual programmes, in addition to its mainstream English programme. This strategy allows it to cater to a broad and diverse expatriate community, aligning with [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate &amp; FDI Inflow Vector]]. The school also demonstrates competitive pricing tactics, such as absorbing GST increases.

## Financial Performance

### FY2017 (Year ended 31 December 2017)
- **Revenue:** S\$96,593,524
- **Profit:** S\$10,373,639
- **Strategic Note:** The company secured a new S\$300 million senior secured term loan facility, indicating major capital investment or refinancing. This substantial loan was secured against a wide range of company assets, highlighting significant financial activity.
