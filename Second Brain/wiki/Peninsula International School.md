# Peninsula International School Australia

## Overview (Historical Baseline Oct 2017)
- **Location:** Setia Alam, Ipoh, and Johor.
- **Land Size:** 3 acres (Setia Alam).
- **Capacity:** 1,300 students.
- **Curriculum:** Victorian Certificate of Education (VCE).
- **Ownership:** Managed by developer (HCK Capital Group).

## Positioning & Marketing
- **Strategy:** "Tier 1 school at Tier 2 fees". Target local residents (middle-high to high income).
- **Founding Promotion:** 30% discount for the first 400 students.
- **Strategic Context:** Direct competitor in the Setia Alam corridor, leveraging aggressive price-to-value positioning to target segments identified in [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]].

## Catchment & Competition
- **Direct Competitors:** [[Tenby Setia Eco Park]], [[AISM]].
- **Catchment Logic:** Primary competition occurs within a 10km radius.
