# Tenby Setia Eco Park

## Overview
A major mid-tier campus located in the high-growth Setia Alam / Bukit Raja corridor. 

## Market Position
- **Anchor Campus:** By 2014, the campus had scaled to 1,900 students, generating RM 36.3m in revenue with a healthy 17.2% PBT margin. 
- **Catchment Saturation:** Acts as the primary K-12 anchor in the area. This hyper-local density is preceded by aggressive early-years competitors like [[Treemendous Education Group]] (TEG), creating a highly competitive funnel for primary intake. 

## Strategic Impact
Validates the rapid maturation of the Setia Alam corridor and the importance of securing early market share in high-growth residential hubs (see [[Brownfield Saturation Limits]]).
