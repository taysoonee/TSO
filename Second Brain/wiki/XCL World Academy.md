### Macro Policy Impact: GST Hike (2023)
- **Observation:** The 2022/2023 fee schedule explicitly accounts for the Singapore GST increase from 7% to 8%, effective January 1, 2023.
- **Strategic Impact:** This tax increase acts as an inflation multiplier for the expatriate demographic in Singapore. This dynamic exacerbates the regional pressure mapped in [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]] and further incentivises the search for high-value cross-border education, aligning with [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate & FDI Inflow Vector]].

### English as an Additional Language (EAL) Fees
- **Level 1 (Enhanced):** 8,000 SGD / year (RM 24,720)
- **Level 2 (Standard):** 5,800 SGD / year (RM 17,922)
- **Mandarin Bilingual Classes:** Incur no additional charge. This is a strategic inclusion to attract the North Asian/Mainland Chinese demographic without pricing friction.
