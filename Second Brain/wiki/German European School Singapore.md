# German European School Singapore

## Overview
GESS is an international school in Singapore offering both the German Curriculum and the IB Curriculum.

## Pricing Shift
- **2019/20 Fees:** As of the 2019/2020 academic year, GESS published fees are inclusive of GST.
    - **Preschool:** Based on choice of 2, 3, or 5 half days per week.
    - **Primary School:** Entrance fee of SGD 2,945 (SGD 2,000 enrolment + SGD 945 non-refundable registration). Annual tuition of SGD 27,450. Development levy of SGD 4,890.
    - **Middle School:** Entrance fee of SGD 2,945. Annual tuition of SGD 31,230. Development levy of SGD 4,890.
    - **High School:** Entrance fee of SGD 2,945. Annual tuition of SGD 34,010. Development levy of SGD 4,890.

## Market Positioning
- GESS has historically shown fee increases higher than the market average (e.g., +3.3% in 2021-22).
- During the historical fee freeze period (2015-2020), GESS was one of the few schools that implemented minor fee increases (+2.7%) compared to the broad stagnation of its peers.
