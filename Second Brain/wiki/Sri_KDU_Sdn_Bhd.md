# Sri KDU Sdn. Bhd.

Sri KDU Sdn. Bhd. is a key Tier-1 competitor in the Malaysian private school market, operating under the [[Sri KDU]] brand.

## Corporate Structure &amp; Finance

### FY2019
For the financial year ended 31 December 2019, the company reported strong financial performance ahead of a major ownership change.
- **Revenue:** RM 91,343,302
- **Profit:** RM 24,439,585
- **Strategic Note:** In January 2020, Prestigion Education Sdn. Bhd. acquired an 80% equity stake from Paramount Corporation Berhad, becoming the new holding company. This acquisition is a significant market event, signaling consolidation within the Tier-1 space. The company also acknowledged the potential negative impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on future demand, a risk factor relevant to both [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate &amp; FDI Inflow Vector]] and [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]].
