# Sri Kuala Lumpur International School (Sri KL)

Sri Kuala Lumpur is an international school tracked for regional fee competitiveness.

## Fees (Academic Year 2026 onwards)
- **Application Fee:** RM 600
- **Registration Fee (Primary):** RM 5,000
- **Registration Fee (Secondary):** RM 7,000
- **Primary Year 1:** RM 22,900 per calendar year.
- **Primary Year 6:** RM 26,900 per calendar year.
- **Secondary Year 7:** RM 33,200 per calendar year.
- **Secondary Year 11:** RM 43,500 per calendar year.

## Update (May 13, 2026): Market Positioning & Competitor Strength
- **Budget/Mid-Tier Dominance:** Insights from the [[Project Falcon]] (March 2023) M&A evaluation identified Sri KL as a strong, dominant competitor in the budget/mid-tier segment. It continues to capture market share from declining legacy brands (like St. John International School), appealing to families impacted by the [[T20 Inflation Squeeze]].

## FY2021 Financial Baseline
- **Tier 2 Market Leadership:** In FYE 2021, Sri KL dominated the Tier 2 local competitor segment, achieving RM 54.91m in net revenue and a segment-leading Profit Before Tax (PBT) of RM 18.97m. 
- **Operational Efficiency:** The school maintained a highly competitive Staff Cost-to-Revenue ratio of 47.0%, significantly outperforming Taylor's TISKL (55.0%). This validates their operational leverage in capturing families affected by the [[T20 Inflation Squeeze]].

## Inbound Migration from Competitors (2018-2021 Data)
Sri KL serves as a key competitor to [[Taylor's International School]] (TISPC campus). Between 2018 and 2021, it was the top destination for departing TISPC students (7 recorded transfers).
