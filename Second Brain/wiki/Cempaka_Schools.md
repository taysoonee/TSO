# Cempaka Schools

Cempaka Schools (via Cempaka Damansara Sdn. Bhd.) is a long-standing competitor in the Malaysian private and international school market.

## Financial Performance

### FY2018 (Year ended 31 July 2018)
- **Revenue:** RM 45,040,618
- **Profit/(Loss):** RM (5,027,554)
- **Strategic Note:** The Group reported a significant net loss, a sharp decline from a net profit of RM 5.4 million in the prior year. This financial downturn could indicate increased competitive pressure or sensitivity to the economic factors outlined in [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]]. The group has also diversified into interactive entertainment services, indicating a potential strategy to broaden its revenue streams.

### FY2017 (7-month period ended 31 July 2017)
- **Revenue (7 months):** RM 15,294,204
- **Profit/(Loss) (7 months):** RM (2,287,842)
- **Strategic Note:** Despite a net loss for the period, the company declared a substantial interim dividend of RM 16 million, suggesting strong backing from its holding company. A significant decrease in 'advance fees and deposits' could indicate future enrolment challenges or a shift in payment patterns, a key metric for monitoring pipeline health. School fees and deposits were assigned as security for bank borrowings, highlighting the critical importance of stable enrolment to its financial structure.
