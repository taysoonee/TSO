# UCSI International School (Kuala Lumpur)

UCSI International School KL is a regional competitor.

## Fees (Academic Year 2026/2027)
- **Application Fee:** RM 1,200
- **Registration Fee:** RM 5,000
- **Year 1:** RM 33,000 per year
- **Year 4 - 6:** RM 38,100 per year
- **Year 7 - 8:** RM 45,300 per year
- **Year 9 - 11:** RM 49,200 per year

Source: [[UCSI KL 2026.pdf]]

- **March 2023:** M&A Committee identified that UCSI International School may be available for sale, with Rothschild investigating the opportunity as part of [[Project Klang Valley Consolidation]].
