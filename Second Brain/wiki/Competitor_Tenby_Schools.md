# Competitor Profile: Tenby World

## Operational Status & Financial Health
Tenby World Sdn. Bhd. underwent structural holding shifts, becoming a subsidiary of Permotio International Learning S.à.r.l. in early 2018. Following this standardisation (shifting financial year-ends to harmonise with the parent organisation), Tenby's 8-month period ending August 2018 generated RM 36.6 million in revenue and RM 7.3 million in profit. This compares favourably against an earlier 12-month baseline in 2016 which saw RM 48.2 million in revenue and RM 7.9 million in profit.

The stable profitability and acquisition by a major international educational fund underscore the consolidation happening in the mid-to-premium tier market, impacting both [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]] and our broader [[Market Intelligence]] baselines.
