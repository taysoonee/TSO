# Hillside World Academy

Hillside World Academy is a competitor in the Singapore international school market, operating as a member of the Huijia Education Group.

## Strategic Positioning &amp; Fee Structure (2019-2020)
- **Curriculum:** Offers the International Baccalaureate (IB) curriculum (PYP, MYP, DP).
- **Target Market:** The school provides trilingual support programmes (English, Chinese, Mother Tongue), indicating a focus on diverse expatriate families. Its connection to the Huijia Education Group in Beijing suggests a strong link to the Chinese market, aligning with the observed 'Chinese Expat Intake' trend and supporting [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate &amp; FDI Inflow Vector]].
- **Fee Structure:** Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$18,500 (Preschool) to S\$33,900 (IB DP), positioning it in the mid-to-high tier of the market. Estimated total annual fees for new students were between S\$23,200 and S\$38,600.
