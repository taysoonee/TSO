# Meeting & Reporting Cadence

This page outlines the governance and operational reporting schedule for [[Taylor's Schools]].

## Quarterly Governance
- **BOG Meeting / BOD Meeting:** 1x/quarter in the last month of the quarter. 
- **BOG/BOD Report:** Due 5 days prior to the meeting.
- **Note:** [[Nexus International School Malaysia]] (NISS) specifically uses the BOD structure.

## Monthly Operations
- **Monthly Management Meeting (MMM):** Usually held in the 3rd week, chaired by the President of Taylor's Schools. Attendees include Principals, HOS, and Finance heads.
- **Monthly Management Report (MMR):** Due by the 10th of each month. This report is a primary feed for [[Market Intelligence]] and funnel tracking.

## Weekly Coordination
- **Huddle:** Every Friday to catch up on projects and assignments.

## Historical Execution Cadence (2010)
- **Tactical Review:** The "Power Planner" framework utilized in 2010 established a quarterly tactical review cycle supported by weekly metric tracking.
- **Priority Alignment:** Each department set **Top 3 Quarterly Priorities**, reinforcing the focus on high-impact objectives.
- **Source:** [[2010 Power Planner - template.xls]]

## Monthly Management Meeting (MMM)
- **Chair:** President of Taylor's Schools.
- **Attendees:** School Principal and key School Management Team staff.
- **Frequency:** Monthly (interspersing with quarterly BOG meetings).
- **Purpose:** Overview of school progress, project updates, highlighting key wins/issues, and requesting approvals for exceptions to standard SOPs/policies.
- **Source:** [[Admin-School Governance and Management- SD_G_ADM_009 - rev2.pdf]]

## Sponsorship Policy Integration
- **[MARKETING] Sponsorship Approvals:** According to the TEG K-12 Sponsorship Policy (SD/P/MKT/002), any intent to engage in external sponsorships (financial, in-kind, or promotional) must be proposed and approved at the **Monthly Management Meeting (MMM)**.
- **Legal Vetting:** Prior to finalization at the MMM, all Sponsorship Agreements must be vetted by the TEG Legal Advisor to ensure there is no conflict of interest and that the partnership aligns with the school's ethos.
