# Lexel

**Lexel** is an affordable international school brand launched in 2026 by [[Taylor's Education Group]] in partnership with Gamuda Land and IDEO.

## Strategic Positioning
Lexel is designed as a "Shield Brand" to capture the rapidly growing mid-market segment (34% CAGR) in the K-12 landscape. By serving the price-sensitive middle class, it mitigates attrition risks highlighted in [[The Living Ledger]] Thesis 2 (Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze) and provides an alternative for families affected by the new [[Taxation & Fiscal Policy]] 6% SST on premium school fees.
