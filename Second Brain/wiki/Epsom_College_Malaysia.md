# Epsom College Malaysia Sdn. Bhd.

Epsom College Malaysia is a competitor in the Malaysian private school market, operating as a preparatory and senior boarding and day school. Its focus on boarding makes it a direct competitor under [[Thesis 3: Boarding as a Premium Utility]].

## Financial Performance

### FY2017
- **Revenue:** RM 38,314,258
- **Profit/(Loss):** RM (13,797,975)
- **Strategic Note:** The college continued to face significant 'going concern' risk due to sustained net losses and current liabilities exceeding assets by RM 227.9 million. It breached its debt service coverage ratio covenants. The company's survival depended on active marketing to grow student intake and continued financial support from its directors.

### FY2016
- **Revenue:** RM 31,490,841
- **Profit/(Loss):** RM (14,472,521)
- **Strategic Note:** The company reported a significant loss and faced material uncertainty about its ability to continue as a going concern, with liabilities exceeding assets by RM 216.5 million. Its operation was dependent on term loan guarantees from its high-profile directors (Tan Sri Tony Fernandes, Datuk Kamarudin Meranun) and an undertaking to cover cash flow shortfalls. The financial distress of such a high-profile brand is a significant market signal regarding the challenges of establishing a new premium school, even with strong backing.
