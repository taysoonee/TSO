## Overview
A strategic ad-hoc project initiated in 2023 within [[Taylor's Schools]].

## Scope
- Strategic undertaking managed by the [[Market Intelligence]] team.
- Aimed at evaluating consolidation opportunities or efficiencies within the Klang Valley campus portfolio.
