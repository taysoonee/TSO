# Competitor Profile: Sri KDU

## Operational Status & Financial Health
Sri KDU Sdn. Bhd. operates as a high-yield asset within the domestic premium education market. In 2019, the group reported a substantial revenue of RM 91.3 million entirely derived from educational fees, converting to a robust net profit of RM 24.4 million. 

This high-profitability model triggered a major corporate action where the paramount holding entity, Paramount Corporation Berhad, divested an 80% equity interest in January 2020. Sri KDU's financial stability and subsequent acquisition reinforce the attractiveness of established domestic-focused international schools, directly correlating with the sustained domestic demand foundational to [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]].
