# Repton Schoolhouse

Repton Schoolhouse is a competitor in the Singapore early years market, targeting children from 18 months to 6 years old.

## Strategic Positioning
Repton positions itself as a premium early years provider with an all-inclusive fee structure covering before/after school care and meals.

### Promotional Activity (2019)
- For 2019, the school ran an aggressive promotional offer, including a 20% discount on termly fees and a full waiver of the registration fee. This indicates a strong drive to capture market share and is a clear example of 'Fee &amp; Bursary Warfare' tactics in the competitive Singapore early years segment. After the discount, the full-day termly fee was reduced from S\$5,500 to S\$4,400 (ex. GST).
