# TEG Code of Conduct

The Code of Conduct and Ethics at Taylor's Education Group (TEG) is designed to maintain high standards of integrity and professionalism.

## Core Values
- **Culture of Excellence:** Continual learning and realization of aspirations.
- **Integrity:** Honesty, accountability, and ethical practice.
- **Passion:** Meaningful and fulfilling work.
- **Respect and Care:** Valuing differences and encouraging initiative.
- **Open Communication:** Frank and sincere exchanges.
- **Enjoyable Environments:** Employee-friendly and supportive.

## Professional Standards
- **Personal Conduct:** Productive and harmonious behavior.
- **Confidentiality:** Protection of "Confidential Information" including computer programs, business finances, and personnel data.
- **Conflict of Interest:** Mandatory disclosure of any financial or personal interests that conflict with the Company.
- **Intellectual Property:** Materials produced during employment using Company resources belong to the Company.

## Employment Policies
- **Probation:** Evaluation of suitability for the position.
- **Performance Appraisal:** Annual cycle from October to September.
- **Retirement:** Age 55.
