# TEG Organizational Structure

Taylor's Education Group (TEG) operates through a network of subsidiaries and educational institutions, governed by **Educrest Sdn. Bhd.** [[Outpatient Policy.pdf]].

## Higher Education
- **Taylor’s University:** Main campus in Subang Jaya [[Outpatient Policy.pdf]].
- **Taylor’s College:** Part of the tertiary education portfolio [[TEG Staff Handbook - April 2012.pdf]].

## Schools (K-12)
The school division has undergone significant rebranding since 2012, notably with Sri Garden becoming Taylor's International School (TIS).
- **Garden International School (GIS):** Includes campuses in Kuala Lumpur and Kuantan [[Outpatient Policy.pdf]].
- **Australian International School Malaysia (AISM):** Located in Seri Kembangan.
- **Nexus International School:** Campuses in Putrajaya (Malaysia) and Singapore.
- **Taylor's International School (TIS):**
    - **TIS Kuala Lumpur (TISKL)**
    - **TIS Puchong (TISPC)**

## Corporate & Support Entities
- **Taylor's Education Sdn. Bhd.**
- **Taylor’s Hostel Management Sdn. Bhd.**
- **TEG Assets Sdn. Bhd.**
- **Quantum Education** (SuperCamp partner).
