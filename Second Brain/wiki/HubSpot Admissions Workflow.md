# HubSpot Admissions Workflow

As part of the Taylor's Schools operational logistics, Admissions relies on an integrated **HubSpot** and **Softr** portal to manage student applications. 

## Academic Application Review Process

The workflow facilitates collaboration between the Admissions Team (Deal Owners / School Tour Leads) and specialized academic reviewers (EYC, Primary, Secondary, EAL, and SEN).

Key process stages and automated system triggers include:
- **Ready for Academic Review:** An automated notification is sent to the relevant Academic (EYC/Primary/Secondary) team to log into the Softr application portal and provide their recommendation within 1 business day.
- **Specialized Reviews:** If an application requires additional scrutiny, notifications are triggered for:
  - **EAL Review:** English as an Additional Language.
  - **SEN Review:** Special Educational Needs.
- **Classroom Observation:** If requested by the academic team, the Deal Owner must arrange an observation with the school. Once completed, the Deal Owner updates "Classroom Obs Comments" and sets the "Classroom Obs Rec" dropdown to "Completed" to notify the academic team.
- **Ready for Offer:** Once all academic team members approve the application, the Deal Owner receives a task to prepare and send the "Formal Letter of Offer" within 1 business day.

## Finance and Application Completion
HubSpot also tracks application completion and payment via automated tasks:
- **Missing Documents / Payment:** School Tour Leads are notified 1 day after submission if documents or the application fee are missing, prompting them to follow up via the online Softr application portal.
- **Payment Notifications:** Real-time emails are triggered upon "Failed" or "Successful" payment attempts.
