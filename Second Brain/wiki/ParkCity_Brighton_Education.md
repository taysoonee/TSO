# ParkCity-Brighton Education Sdn. Bhd.

ParkCity-Brighton Education Sdn. Bhd. operates a private international school in the affluent Desa ParkCity township in Kuala Lumpur, making it a key competitor in a prime catchment area.

## Financial Performance

### FY2018 (Year ended 30 June 2018)
- **Revenue:** RM 60,915,408
- **Profit/(Loss):** RM (871,771)
- **Strategic Note:** The company reported a loss, primarily due to significant finance costs (RM 13.2 million) and a large non-current receivable from its immediate holding company, Perdana ParkCity Sdn. Bhd. This indicates a complex financial structure heavily integrated with its parent property developer. Despite the loss, a dividend of RM 2.48 million was paid, suggesting confidence from the parent company. The school's location in Desa ParkCity makes its performance a key indicator for hyper-local market dynamics under [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]].
