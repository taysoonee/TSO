# Taylor's Schools Growth Strategy 2018
\n## Key Strategic Pillars
\n- **Mission Statement (2016-2025):** To be a premier global K-12 education provider and to lay the foundation for lifelong success for our learners.
\nThis document outlines the growth strategy for Taylor's Schools as of 2018, extracted from a presentation.
\n- **Market Competition:** As of June 2016, there was a noted increase in the number of international schools in Malaysia, indicating a more competitive landscape. This aligns with [[Competitor Analysis]] and [[Market Intelligence]].
\n\n## Market & Growth
\n- **Historical Performance (2005-2018):** The group saw a 161% increase in enrolment and a 759% increase in revenue.
\n- **Global Expansion:** The strategy includes plans for multiple campuses for [[Garden International School]], [[Taylor's International School]], [[Australian International School Malaysia]], and [[Nexus International School Malaysia]]. It also mentions an advisory service for international curriculum mission schools.
\n- The use of SuperCamp residential camps and parenting workshops were noted as potential channels for [[Admissions & Operations|student recruitment]].
\n\n## Lead Generation
\n- **[[Taylor's International School]] Model:** This model is positioned as a mid-price offering, primarily employing local teachers for local students. It is designed to be a franchisable and scalable brand with centralized administration.
