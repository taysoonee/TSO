# Sri Bestari International School

Sri Bestari is a key competitor in the 'Affordable Premium' segment, located in Bandar Sri Damansara. It is operated by **Lang Education Sdn Bhd**, a subsidiary of [[Land & General Berhad]].

## Market Positioning
- **Segment:** Mid-tier Value-Cambridge.
- **Differentiator:** Digital Maker Hub status (MDEC) and strong focus on STEM/Global Best Practices.
- **Curriculum:** Dual-stream (Private/National and International).

## Financial Performance (2020 Baseline)
- **Operator:** Lang Education Sdn Bhd.
- **Revenue:** RM 19.56 million.
- **EBITDA:** RM 5.89 million (30% margin).
- **Net Profit After Tax:** RM 1.63 million (8% margin).
- **Enrolment:** ~1,400 students (57% utilization of 2,450 capacity).

## Strategic Linkage
- **Acquisition Target:** Evaluated by [[Taylor's Schools]] in 2021 as part of [[Project TISPJ]] for potential rebranding.
- **Competitive Pressure:** Directly competes with [[Taylor's International School Kuala Lumpur|Taylor's International School KL]] and [[Taylor's International School Puchong|Taylor's International School Puchong]] for families seeking high-value international education in the PJ/Damansara corridor.
