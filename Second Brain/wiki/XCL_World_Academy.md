# XCL World Academy

XCL World Academy (formerly [[GEMS World Academy Singapore]]) is a premium international school in Singapore, offering the full IB continuum.

## Strategic Positioning
The school differentiates itself by having two qualified teachers in every class, offering personalised learning, and providing proven pathways to global higher education. It also promotes a diverse community and world-class facilities.

### Market Strategy
- **Scholarships:** The school offers scholarships covering 25%-100% of tuition fees, awarding an average of S\$450,000 per year over the last five years. This is a key competitive tool to attract high-achieving students.
- **Sibling Discounts:** Offers 10% discount for the 1st/2nd sibling and 15% for the 3rd+, a strategy to attract and retain multi-child families.
- **Payment Plans:** Provides 0% interest-free instalment plans via bank partners, increasing financial accessibility.
- **Bilingual Programme:** Offers a Chinese-English bilingual programme, catering to demand from specific expatriate demographics and local families. This is a key market differentiator relevant to the [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate &amp; FDI Inflow Vector]].

### Fee Structure (2023-2024)
- **Annual Tuition Fees (excl. GST):** Range from S\$22,684 (Nursery) to S\$43,256 (IBDP).
- **GST Impact:** The school notes the GST increase from 8% to 9% in January 2024, a macro factor impacting affordability.
- **Application Fee (excl. GST):** S\$850
- **Enrolment Fee (excl. GST):** S\$5,200
