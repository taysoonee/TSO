# Bandar Serai (Gamuda)

## Overview
Bandar Serai is a 727.3-acre township development by Gamuda located in Kuang, Rawang. 

## Market Intelligence (June 2026)
- **Status:** Potential site for school expansion.
- **Land Details:** 727.3 acres leasehold land purchased for RM 620 million (approx. RM 20 psf).
- **Project Scope:** Mixed development with 2,903 units.
- **Catchment Area:** Rawang (200k), Sungai Buloh (466k), Selayang (285k).
- **Strategic Advantage:** Potential first-mover advantage at the edge of town with high connectivity via North-South Expressway, Guthrie Corridor, and LATAR Highway.
