# Singapore Foreign System School (FSS) RFI 2024\n\n**Date:** 31 January 2024\n**Issuer:** Economic Development Board (EDB) Singapore\n\n## Overview\nThe 2024 RFI is the latest government exercise to allocate State land for the establishment of Foreign System Schools (FSS) to meet the educational needs of the expatriate community in Singapore.\n\n## Designated Sites\n1. **Hougang Avenue 3** (30-year lease)\n2. **Gambas Crescent** (30-year lease)\n\n## Critical Pre-requisites\n- **Student Profile:** Minimum 80% enrolment from Dependant Pass Holders (DPH), Immigration Exemption Order Holders (IEOH), or Student Pass holders (STP) with valid diplomatic/work pass parents.\n- **Community Integration:** Inclusion of social integration and community engagement in the curriculum.\n- **Facility Sharing:** Agreement to share school facilities with the general public regularly.\n- **Traffic Management:** Comprehensive traffic management plan required to mitigate impact on local residents.\n- **Public Communication:** Mandatory public communications plan (Annex F) to engage with residents within a 100m radius.\n\n## Evaluation Criteria (Weightage)\n- **Quality (50%):** Track record, governance, curriculum (breadth/depth), academic results, staff quality.\n- **Ability to Meet Market Demand (25%):** Target market, enrolment ramp-up, fees, diversity.\n- **Financial Viability (25%):** Investment amount, financing ability, breakeven timeline.\n\n## Strategic Implications for Taylor's Schools\n- **Competitive Threat:** Allocation of these sites will introduce new premium capacity into the market, potentially impacting [[Nexus International School Singapore]]'s market share.\n- **Expansion Opportunity:** As a past RFI winner, Taylor's Schools has the institutional capability to bid for these sites if they align with the [[Project Lisa]] or regional growth strategy.\n- **Expat Focus:** The 80% quota validates [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate & FDI Inflow Vector]] and the government's commitment to supporting the FDI-driven expat population.\n\n## Related\n- [[Singapore K-12 Market]]\n- [[Nexus International School Singapore]]\n- [[Competitor Analysis]]\n- [[Thesis ## New Tender Sites (2024 RFI)
- **Closing Date:** 5 July 2024
- **Gambas Crescent:** 1.8 hectares, Foreign System School, target enrolment 1,500 students. Located in Sembawang Planning Area.
- **Hougang Avenue 3:** 2.0 hectares, Foreign System School, target enrolment 1,800 students. Located in Hougang Planning Area.
- **Strategic Context:** Both sites require Traffic Impact Assessments (TIA), specific PUDO driveway provisions, and signalised pedestrian crossing enhancements (Annex A). NEA requirements include noise mitigation for high ambient noise areas (Annex B).
