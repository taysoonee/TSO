# BK Gan

BK Gan is the President of [[Taylor's Schools]], the K-12 division of [[Taylor's Education Group]].

## Leadership & Governance
- **Role:** Leads the Schools Office and oversees the performance of the entire K-12 portfolio, including [[Garden International School]], [[Nexus International School]], [[Australian International School Malaysia]], and [[Taylor's International School]].
- **Reporting:** Historically part of the Group Management Committee (GMC) and reports to the Group CEO, Dato' Loy Teik Ngan.
- **Strategic Focus:** Under his leadership, the Market Intelligence function was centralized to report directly to him (interim) in 2023, emphasizing a data-driven approach to growth and M&A.

## Update (May 14, 2026): ESG Leadership Mandate
- **Senior Most Executive (ESG):** Formal corporate reporting records from 2022 explicitly designate BK Gan as the "Senior Most Executive (ESG)" for the division. This dual role underscores the integration of [[ESG]] compliance and sustainability directly into the President's executive mandate, bridging operational initiatives (like the solar rollout) with high-level stakeholder reporting (e.g., to entities like [[KKR (Kohlberg Kravis Roberts)]]).
