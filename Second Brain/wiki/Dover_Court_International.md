# Dover Court International School (Pte.) Ltd.

## The Nord Anglia Engine
Acquired and restructured by Nord Anglia Education Inc. (subsequently routed via Bach Acquisitions Limited), Dover Court has streamlined into a highly efficient S-tier generator. Their operational delta is expanding rapidly: climbing from S$ 33.4 million top-line (2017) to S$ 38.6 million (2018), achieving nearly S$ 9.5 million in net yields.

## Royalty & Capital Extractions
Nord Anglia enforces a highly engineered capital extraction routine via internal royalty fees paid cross-subsidiary. In addition to primary tuition (S$ 36.5M in 2018), Dover Court successfully operationalises non-refundable Building Fund levies (clearing over S$ 2 million annually) which directly offset their S$ 544k operating lease commitments.
