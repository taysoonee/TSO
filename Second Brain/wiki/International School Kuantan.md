# International School Kuantan (ISK)

International School Kuantan (ISK) is a direct competitor to [[Garden International School]] (GIS) in the Kuantan market. 

## Market Position
- As of 2012, ISK was the primary destination for students leaving GIS Kuantan after Year 8, as it offered Secondary education which GIS Kuantan lacked at the time.
- GIS estimated a leakage of approximately 5 students per year to ISK prior to the 2013 extension of GIS Year 9 offering.

## Strategic Context
- The presence of ISK was a key driver for the GIS Kuantan expansion and the eventual feasibility study for [[Project TIS Kuantan]].
