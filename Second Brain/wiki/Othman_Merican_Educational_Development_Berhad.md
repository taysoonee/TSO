# Othman Merican Educational Development Berhad

Othman Merican Educational Development Berhad operates as a private primary and secondary school in Malaysia.

## Financial Performance &amp; Strategy

### FY2018
- **Revenue:** RM 47,368,039
- **Profit:** RM 9,433,262
- **Strategic Note:** The company operates as a non-profit oriented school and is tax-exempt. This provides a significant competitive advantage, allowing it to either offer more competitive fees or reinvest more of its surplus into facilities and programs. This tax status is a key consideration when analysing market dynamics under [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]], as it can better absorb cost pressures.
