# Project RFI 2024

This project tracks the Request for Information (RFI) issued in 2024.

## Annex D: Sample State Lease Agreement

A sample State Lease Agreement was provided as part of the RFI information pack. This document outlines the standard terms and conditions for the lease of state land for educational purposes.

### Key Commercial Terms

*   **Lessor**: President of the Republic of Singapore.
*   **Lease Term**: 30 years.
*   **Permitted Use**: The land is to be used exclusively for the development and operation of a single pre-tertiary educational institution registered as a foreign system school with the Economic Development Board.
*   **Premium**: An "Alienation Premium" is payable upfront. The specific amount is not stated in the sample.
*   **Additional Premiums**:
    *   **Additional Land Premium**: Payable if the development's gross plot ratio exceeds the stipulated intensity. The amount is determined by the Chief Valuer.
    *   **Land Betterment Charge**: Payable if the Lessor grants consent to vary the land use.
*   **Operating Covenants**:
    *   **Taxes & Outgoings**: The Lessee is responsible for all property taxes, rates, and other impositions.
    *   **Maintenance**: The Lessee must maintain the land, buildings, and fixtures in a "good and tenantable state".
    *   **Assignment & Subletting**: The Lessee cannot assign, mortgage, sublet, or part with possession of the land without the Lessor's prior written consent. An exception is made for mortgaging to a licensed bank or finance company in Singapore.
*   **End of Lease**:
    *   The Lessee must surrender the land and all buildings in good and tenantable condition.
    *   There is no compensation to the Lessee for the buildings or improvements.
    *   The Lessor can require the Lessee, at the Lessee's own cost, to remove all structures and restore the land.
*   **Governing Law**: The lease is governed by the State Lands Act 1920 and State Lands Rules 1993.

This sample agreement provides a baseline for the legal and financial obligations associated with leasing state land for school development under this RFI.
