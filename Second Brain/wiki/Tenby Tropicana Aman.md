# Tenby Tropicana Aman\n\nTenby Tropicana Aman is an international school campus associated with the Tropicana/Dijaya group.\n\n## Financial Performance (2020 vs 2019)\n- **Revenue:** RM 5.7 million (a rapid increase of 100.4% from 2019).\n- **Loss Before Tax:** -RM 1.8 million (loss reduced by 76.3% from 2019).\n- **Accumulated Losses:** RM 11.9 million.\n- **Strategic Implication:** The 100.4% revenue growth indicates a successful early-stage ramp-up in enrolment. However, the campus still operates at a loss. The overall financial drag of the Tropicana portfolio (including [[GEMS Metropark]] and [[St. Joseph's Institution International School Malaysia]]) may limit their ability to sustain aggressive marketing or bursary strategies in the mid-tier segment.\n- **Source:** [[Dijaya's Schools.pptx]]\n\nRelated: [[Competitor Analysis]], [[Tenby International School]]
