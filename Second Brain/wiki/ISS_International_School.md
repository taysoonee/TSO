# ISS International School

ISS International School is a competitor in the Singapore premium international education market.

## Fee Structure &amp; Strategy
ISS employs several promotional strategies to attract and retain students, including early-bird discounts, family discounts, and referral programmes.

- **2021-2022:** Total annual course fees ranged from S\$25,188 (K1-K2) to S\$41,354 (G12). The school offered a 5% early-bird discount and a significant 'Family Fee Programme' (20% discount and waived enrolment fee for 3rd child onwards).
[ARCHIVE: ISS - 2021.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/ISS/2021]

- **2020-2021:** Total annual course fees ranged from S\$25,188 to S\$41,354. The school also offered a postal code catchment zone discount (3%) for specific areas, a hyper-local strategy relevant to [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]]. A refer-a-friend programme offered a S\$2,500 credit.
[ARCHIVE: ISS - 2020.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/ISS International School/2020]

- **2019-2020:** Total annual course fees ranged from S\$28,422 to S\$41,354.
[ARCHIVE: ISS - 2019.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/ISS International School/2019-2020]

- **2018-2019:** Total annual course fees ranged from S\$25,190 to S\$40,220.
[ARCHIVE: ISS - 2018.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/ISS International School/2018]

- **2014-2015:** Annual tuition fees (with GST) ranged from S\$23,840 to S\$33,833.
