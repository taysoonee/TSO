# Canadian International School (CIS)
**Jurisdiction:** Singapore
**Corporate Entity:** Canadian International School Pte Ltd (wholly-owned by Star Readers Pte Ltd)

## Financial & Operational Baselines

### 2017
- **Revenue:** S$ 96,593,524.
- **Profit:** S$ 10,373,639.
- **Strategic Notes:** Secured a massive senior term loan facility of S$ 300M to settle previous banking structures and underwrite infrastructure development. 

### 2019–2020
- **Enrolment:** Approximately 3,300 students.
- **Fees:** S$ 2,500 application fee benchmark.
- **Strategic Notes:** Positioned competitively via IB curricula across two distinct campuses. Deploys a 'schools-within-a-school' model at the Lakeside campus, alongside a dedicated STEAM makerspace and a bilingual Chinese-English programme. This product differentiation actively target shifting demographics observed in [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate & FDI Inflow Vector]].
