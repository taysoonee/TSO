# Charterhouse Malaysia

Charterhouse Malaysia is an emerging premium competitor in the K-12 landscape, primarily focusing on A-Level and senior school education.

## Competitive Threat to Taylor's Schools
- **FY2022 Surge:** Analysis of [[Garden International School]] leaver data reveals that Charterhouse Malaysia became the top domestic destination for GIS withdrawals in 2022. It successfully captured 12 GIS students, overtaking traditional Tier 1 competitors like ISKL and Alice Smith.
- **Strategic Implication:** The successful poaching of GIS students indicates strong positioning in the A-Level and pre-university segment, posing a direct threat to the senior school retention at Taylor's Tier 1 assets.
