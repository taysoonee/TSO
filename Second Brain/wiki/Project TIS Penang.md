# Project TIS Penang

In November 2016, Taylor's Schools presented a **Gate 2** approach proposal to the **[[Investment Committee]]** for setting up a TIS model school in Penang.

## Project Overview
- **Location:** 4 acres of land in Queens Waterfront, Penang, owned by Ideal Properties Group.
- **Capacity:** 640 students (Phase 1).
- **Target Opening:** January 2020 (subject to developer negotiations).

## Deal Economics (Scenario 3a)
- **Initial Outlay:** RM3.7 million.
- **Working Capital:** RM7.7 million (over 4 years).
- **Total Funding:** RM11.4 million.
- **Breakeven:** 525 students (Year 4).
- **Payback Period:** Year 12.
- **IRR:** 4.6% (Year 15).

## Strategic Assessment
- **Pros:** Entry into the Penang market, strategic location, acceptable investment return.
- **Cons:** MOE still issuing licenses (at the time); requirement for a demographic study to include total population of Penang Island and Chinese Independent High Schools.

## Gate Gap Due Diligence Updates
- **Gate 1 Financial Revisions:** IRR projected at 1.7% (10 years) and 18.2% (20 years). Breakeven estimated at Year 6 (533 students) with a payback period in Year 9. Developer seeks long-term lease with an indicative rate of RM1.65 to RM1.80 psf per month (GFA). 
- **Gate 2 Metrics:** Revised investment parameters include a RM 6.38 million capital outlay and RM 10.62 million in working capital over 4 years, yielding an 8-year payback period for a 2-phase development (640 students per phase).
- **Market Dynamics:** Penang's international school penetration rate sits at a low 1.5% (contrasting with Klang Valley's 6%). While established international schools in Penang operate at around 80% capacity, new entrants within a 10km radius are struggling at <50% capacity.
- **Partnership Structure:** Property owned 100% by Ideal Property Group (Dato’ Alex Ooi Kee Liang, Chairman), with the school operating entity 100% owned by [[Taylor's Education Group]] (TEG). Ideal has signed an SPA with the state government, but an MOE license application is pending.
