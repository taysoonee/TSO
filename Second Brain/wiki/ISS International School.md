*Analysis of historical data indicates a significant escalation in both pricing and marketing aggressiveness.*

- **Enrolment Fee:** $3,600 SGD (~RM11,124)
- **Development Fee:** $2,460 SGD (~RM7,601) for G6-G12.

### Tactical Evolution (Marketing & Admissions)
- **Referral Bounties:** In 2018-2019, the referral bounty was a modest $500 SGD one-time discount. By 2020, this escalated aggressively to a $2,500 SGD credit for *both* the referring family and the new enrollee. This massive 5x increase signals significant friction in organic lead generation and a pivot towards high-cost network acquisition.
- **Family Fee Programme (2018):** Required 3 or more children. The 3rd and subsequent child received an Enrolment Fee waiver and a 20% discount on Tuition & Development fees.
- **Late Penalties:** Enforced a harsh 8% *per month* interest rate on late payments up to 2 months. 

*Strategic Impact:* The structural fee hikes between 2018 and 2020 (+10% in high school), combined with the dramatic increase in acquisition costs (Referral Bounties), highlights the high-pressure environment of the [[Singapore K-12 Market]]. This contrast elevates the attractiveness of Malaysian premium schools as a cost-effective alternative for expatriates relocating to the region ([[Thesis 1: The Expatriate & FDI Inflow Vector]]).
