# Mont' Kiara International School (MKIS)

Mont' Kiara International School (MKIS) is a key Tier-1 competitor located in the prime Mont' Kiara corridor in Kuala Lumpur.

## Financial Performance

### FY2017 (Year ended 31 July 2017)
- **Revenue:** RM 58,110,034
- **Profit:** RM 199,126
- **Strategic Note:** MKIS experienced a drastic decline in performance, with revenue falling from RM 72.2M in FY2016 and profit plummeting from RM 6.9M. This sharp downturn is a significant market signal and could have triggered an `[EXECUTIVE ALERT]`. The trend strongly supports [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]], suggesting even affluent families are reconsidering high-cost education. It also points to intense hyper-local competition in the Mont' Kiara catchment area, validating the need for systematic competitor surveillance as per [[The Living Ledger]]. A decrease in 'fees billed in advance' from RM 28.1M to RM 23.8M suggests potential future enrolment weakness.
