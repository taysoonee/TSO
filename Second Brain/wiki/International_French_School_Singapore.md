# International French School (Singapore)

The International French School (Singapore) is a key competitor in the Singapore market, catering to the French and international community. It is a member of the 'aefe' network (Agency for French Education Abroad).

## Fee Structure &amp; Governance
The school's fees are determined annually by a General Assembly involving parent representatives, indicating a community-governed approach. It offers significant large family discounts and multiple academic streams.

- **2023-2024:** Full-year tuition fees ranged from S\$11,730 (Toute Petite Section) to S\$30,120 (High School), with surcharges for specialised streams like 'Anglais Plus' and 'International Section'. The fee schedule clearly reflects the impending GST increase from 8% to 9%. Discounts of 5-15% are offered for the 3rd child onwards.
