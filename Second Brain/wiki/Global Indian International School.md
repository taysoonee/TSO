## Strategic Context
GIIS is a major competitor in the [[Singapore K-12 Market]], particularly known for its technology-integrated 'SMART' campus and competitive fee structure compared to premium tier-1 schools. Its parent company, Global Schools Holdings, also oversees a network of schools globally.
