# FOBISIA (Federation of British International Schools in Asia)

FOBISIA is a regional federation of premium British international schools in Asia. Membership is a significant brand signal for 'Premium British' education and provides access to regional sporting, music, and academic competitions.

## Strategic Significance
- **Competitor Benchmark:** Many of Taylor's Schools' direct competitors are members (e.g., [[Garden International School]], [[Alice Smith International School]], [[Epsom College Malaysia]]).
- **Accreditation Signal:** Membership requires a rigorous pre-membership visit and ongoing commitment to British educational standards.

## Recent Activity
- **St. Joseph's Institution International Malaysia (SJIIM):** Recommended for Associate Membership in November 2022 following a pre-membership visit.
