# UWC South East Asia (UWCSEA)
**Jurisdiction:** Singapore

## Financial & Operational Baselines

### 2012–2013
- **Revenue:** S$ 147,490,109 (Dover: S$ 89.5M, East: S$ 57.9M). Tuition constitutes roughly 89% - 91% of total income across campuses.
- **Profit:** S$ 9,249,360 combined (Dover: S$ 3.7M, East: S$ 5.4M).
- **Enrolment:** 4,909 students total (Dover: 2,997, East: 2,404).
- **Strategic Notes:** Operates on a charitable trust model with heavy philanthropic engagement via the Foundation. The East Campus underwent rapid capacity expansion. UWCSEA initiated a major curriculum articulation project, pivoting away from the IB PYP in Infant/Junior levels. Known for highly competitive global university placement metrics (99% acceptance) and significant boarding integration at the Dover campus.
