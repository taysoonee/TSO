# Kingsley International School

Kingsley International School (operated by Kingsley International Sdn. Bhd.) is a key competitor in the Malaysian international school market.

## Financial Performance &amp; Expansion

### FY2019 (Year ended 30 June 2019)
- **Revenue:** RM 28,871,740
- **Profit:** RM 6,186,872
- **Strategic Note:** The company undertook significant property, plant, and equipment acquisitions (RM 20.5M), signaling active campus development and expansion. This aligns with monitoring competitor movements under [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]]. The complex international holding structure (BVI, HK Stock Exchange) suggests a strong link to foreign investment, relevant to [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate &amp; FDI Inflow Vector]].

### FY2018 (Year ended 30 June 2018)
- **Revenue:** RM 30,066,205
- **Profit:** RM 11,835,791
- **Strategic Note:** The balance sheet reflected substantial ongoing facility development, with RM 44.8 million in 'Building under construction' and RM 10.6 million in capital commitments.

### FY2017 (Year ended 30 June 2017)
- **Revenue:** RM 26,850,396
- **Profit:** RM 11,423,767
- **Strategic Note:** Reported significant growth in revenue and profit. Approved capital commitments for property and equipment amounted to RM 26 million. The company also acquired a 70% interest in Kingsley Language House Sdn. Bhd., indicating diversification into related educational services.

### FY2016 (Year ended 30 June 2016)
- **Revenue:** RM 18,046,703
- **Profit:** RM 2,323,211
- **Strategic Note:** Underwent a significant capital and ownership restructuring. Its holding company ceased to be B&G Capital Resources Berhad, with shares transferred to a director, Tan Sri Dato' Sri Goh Ming Choon. This change in control is a significant event for competitive analysis, reflecting potential strategic repositioning.
