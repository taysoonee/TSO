# Glossary

This glossary provides definitions for commonly used terms and acronyms within Taylor's Education Group (TEG) [[TS Glossary of Terms.pdf]].

## Governance & Leadership
- **GMC:** Group Management Committee (formerly TEG EXCO).
- **BOA / BOD / BOG:** Board of Advisors / Directors / Governors.
- **HOS:** Head of School.
- **Corporate HR / GHR:** Group Human Resources based at the Corporate Office.

## Project Management & Operations
- **2-Page / 2-Page PMT:** The standard Project Management Tool used within the group.
- **ACE:** Awesome Customer Experience.
- **CPD:** Central Purchasing Department.
- **eDP / KnowledgeTree:** Electronic Document Portal (document management system).
- **Huddle:** Weekly Power Planner meeting for the School Division.
- **SpA Meeting:** Strategic Priorities Alignment Meeting (bi-annual).
- **PP:** Power Planner (strategic and tactical planning tool).

## Campus & Locations
- **Lakeside:** Taylor's University Lakeside Campus.
- **LCS:** Leisure Commerce Square (Corporate Office location for Group HR, Audit, and CPD).
- **1MK:** 1 Mont Kiara (Location for Taylor's Schools Office).

## Academic & Enrollment
- **EFTS:** Equivalent Full Time Student.
- **Student Attrition:** Students leaving the school for reasons other than graduation.
- **Student Migration:** Specific tracking of students moving between group schools (e.g., SG to International).

## Medical & Insurance
- **ASO:** Administrative Services Only (refers to the outpatient medical benefit management where the employer pays claims directly while the insurer handles administration) [[Outpatient Policy.pdf]].
- **GHP:** Group Health Plan (Inpatient and Surgical insurance coverage) [[Inpatient Policy.pdf]].
- **LOG:** Letter of Guarantee (issued by the insurer to a hospital to guarantee payment of medical expenses, enabling cashless admission) [[Outpatient Policy.pdf]].
- **MY AIA:** The mobile application used by staff for electronic identification (e-Member ID) and outpatient claims management.
- **Preferred Care Program:** The specific AIA program name for TEG's outpatient medical management.
- **RB:** Room & Board (Daily allowance for hospital room charges).

## Meeting & Reporting Cadence
- **MMM:** Monthly Management Meeting (Usually 3rd week of the month).
- **MMR:** Monthly Management Report (Due on the 10th of every month).
- **Huddle:** Weekly meeting on Fridays for project updates.
- **BOG Chairperson:** Tan Sri Alimuddin.

## Human Resources & Performance
- **TPF:** [[Taylor's Performance Framework]].
- **ePerformance:** Automated goal-setting and appraisal system built on [[Cornerstone]].
- **Cornerstone:** The cloud-based Human Capital Management (HCM) software used for staff performance and learning.
- **R.E.C.I.P.E.:** Taylor's Core Values (Respect, Excellence, Communication, Integrity, Passion, Enjoyment).
