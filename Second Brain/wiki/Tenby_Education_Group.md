# Tenby Schools (Tenby World Sdn. Bhd.)

## Market Infiltration Vector
Tenby has weaponised an "affordable fee" pricing structure specifically engineered to intercept the middle-upper tier demographic currently besieged by macro-economic tightening ([[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]]). Across 2017, the network reported RM 53.4 million in revenue, locking in RM 11.1 million in profit.

## Campus Saturation Checks
With operating nodes stretching from Ipoh and Penang down through Miri, their critical frontline intersection with Taylor's footprint exists at their Setia Eco Park campus (approximately 1,400 students). Their presence here dramatically intensifies local threat levels when factored alongside hyper-local competitors like [[Treemendous Education Group]], effectively weaponizing [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]].
