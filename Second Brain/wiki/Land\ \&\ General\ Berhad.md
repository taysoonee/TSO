# Land & General Berhad

Land & General Berhad (L&G) is a diversified property developer in Malaysia with a significant presence in the education sector through its subsidiary, **Lang Education Sdn Bhd**.

## Education Portfolio
- **[[Sri Bestari International School]]**: Operates private and international schools in Bandar Sri Damansara.
- **Sri Bestari Private School**: National curriculum stream.
- **Sri Bestari Kindergarten**.

## Strategic Importance
- **M&A Target:** The education arm was a subject of acquisition interest by [[Taylor's Schools]] in 2021 ([[Project TISPJ]]).
- **Growth Hub:** L&G's development of the Bandar Sri Damansara and PJ corridors provides a captive demographic for its schools.
