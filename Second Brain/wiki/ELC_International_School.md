# ELC International School (Zaum Sdn. Bhd.)

## Operational Efficiency & Profit Extraction
Managed by Zaum Sdn. Bhd., ELC International School demonstrates highly efficient capital extraction and sustained profitability. The entity declared a massive net dividend of RM 12.5M in 2012. By 2015, gross revenues breached RM 22.2M, yielding a RM 5.5M net profit. ELC maintains strong treasury reserves (RM 26.7M in 2015) whilst actively driving down pledged property assets.

## Market Positioning
Their ability to consistently extract high net profit percentages whilst retaining capital depth makes them a durable legacy competitor. Their drastically escalating staff costs (rising from RM 8.8M to RM 11.5M by 2015) mirror the broader industry talent retention challenges and expatriate demands, although their lean capital structure shields them effectively from domestic macroeconomic friction noted in [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]].
