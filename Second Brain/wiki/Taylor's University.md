# Taylor's University

Taylor's University is a leading private university in Malaysia, located in Subang Jaya.

## Staff Benefits
Staff are covered under the group-wide [[TEG Benefits]] framework, including the **AIA Group Health Plan** and **Preferred Care Program** for 2025.

**Source:** [[Outpatient Policy.pdf]]

## Infrastructure and ESG Initiatives
In September 2020, the university explored significant sustainability investments, receiving an EPCC proposal for a 779.24 kWp Solar Photovoltaic System from Plus Solar Systems. The project represented a CAPEX of RM 1,839,000.00 but projected an estimated Net Savings of RM 8,765,343.18 over 25 years, with a fast payback period of 3.03 years. This initiative aligns with the group's broader [[ESG]] strategy and preceded similar sustainable rollouts across the [[Taylor's Schools]] portfolio.

### Update (May 14, 2026): Solar PV Infrastructure (2021)
