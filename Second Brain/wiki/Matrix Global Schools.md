# Matrix Global Schools\n\n## Overview\nMatrix Global Schools (operated by Matrix Global Education Sdn Bhd) is a competitor based in Negeri Sembilan.\n\n## Leadership & Legal Risks\n- **Constructive Dismissal Case (2018):** Former CEO Felix Lee Eng Boon successfully claimed constructive dismissal after being removed from his position and reassigned to a marketing role at headquarters without proper justification or training. The Industrial Court found the company failed to prove the dismissal was with just cause. This incident highlights historical leadership instability and serves as a case study for executive employment risks in the sector. Linked to [[Risk Management]] and [[Competitor Analysis]].
