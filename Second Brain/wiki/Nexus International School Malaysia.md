# Nexus International School Malaysia (NISM)

Nexus International School Malaysia (NISM) is a premium international school within the Taylor's Education Group portfolio. It is classified as a Tier 1 asset in the [[Competitor Analysis]].

## Admissions Data
Based on a 2025/2026 Year 10 offer letter, key admission and financial details include:
- **Promotional Waivers:** An Open Day promotion in August 2025 offered 50% off registration fees.
- **Deposit Policy:** An equivalent of one term's tuition fee is required as a deposit. The amount is scaled as tuition fees increase by year level. Parents must provide ONE (1) full academic term’s written notice for withdrawal to receive a refund.
- **International Payments:** NISM utilizes Flywire for international transactions, allowing payments via Visa, Mastercard, UnionPay, and local bank transfers.
- **Community:** Enrolling families automatically become members of the Parent Teacher Friends group (PTF).

**Source:** [[NISM offer letter.pdf]]
