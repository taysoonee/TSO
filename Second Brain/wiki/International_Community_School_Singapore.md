# International Community School (Singapore)

International Community School (Singapore) Ltd is a competitor in the Singapore international school market, operating as a company limited by guarantee and a registered charity. It offers an American-based, college preparatory curriculum from Pre-Kindergarten to Grade 12, framed by Christian values.

## Strategic Positioning
ICS differentiates itself through a focus on Christian values, small class sizes, and comparatively moderate fees within the premium segment. This positions it to attract families from business, ministry, military, and diplomatic backgrounds. It also provides targeted financial assistance to parents in full-time Christian service.

- **FY2021 Analysis:** The school froze tuition fees for the 2020-2021 academic year in response to market conditions, demonstrating sensitivity to family financial pressures. It also introduced a 10% sibling discount for the third child onwards.
[ARCHIVE: ICS - 2021.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/International Community School/2021]

- **FY2020 Analysis:** The school served over 400 students from more than 25 countries, with a significant cohort from the USA (31%) and South Korea. Its value proposition includes all-inclusive pricing for textbooks, supplies, and technology (1:1 devices).
[ARCHIVE: ICS - 2020.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/International Community School (Singapore)/2020]

## Financial Performance &amp; Expansion Plans
The school's financials demonstrate steady operation with a consistent focus on funding future campus expansion.

### FY2018 (Year ended 30 June 2018)
- **Revenue:** S\$11,791,197
- **Profit (Surplus):** S\$390,754
- **Strategic Note:** The school maintained several designated funds, including a Capital Fund for new buildings and a Special Projects Fund for a new campus project. This earmarking of funds indicates a clear, long-term expansion strategy, relevant to [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]].
