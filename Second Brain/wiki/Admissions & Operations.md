## Update (May 12, 2026): TM1 Operational Formulas
The [[School Management System Integration (SMSI)]] provides standardized formulas for group-wide reporting:

### Enrolment Metrics
- **Total Enrolled Student:** `Continuing Student + New Student + New Student Adj (Same Month Attrition)`.
- **New Student:** Matching month/year of 'date of entry' (Engage) or 'admission date' (SIMS) with the report refresh date.
- **Attrition Student:** Matching month/year of 'leaving date' with report refresh date.

### Retention & Progression Tracking
Retention is measured on an academic year basis across three primary transition points:
1. **Primary Transition:** Reception/Kindergarten to Year 1/Foundation.
2. **Secondary Transition:** Year 6 to Year 7.
3. **Tertiary/Post-16 Transition:** Year 11 to Year 12 (A-Level/IB/HSC).

### Student Lifetime Analysis
- **Average Student Lifetime (Years):** `Total Student Lifetime (Years) / Total Attrition and Graduate`.
- **Average Student Lifetime (Revenue):** `Total Student Lifetime Revenue / Total Attrition and Graduate`. Used to assess the long-term ROI of acquisition and the impact of [[T20 Inflation Squeeze]].

Source: [[Taylors_SMSI _User Training Manual_v1.2.pdf]]

## Update (May 13, 2026): E-Wallet Vendor Evaluation (Vircle vs CALMS)
- **Evaluation Matrix:** Vircle was identified as the superior e-wallet solution over CALMS, scoring 87% vs 58%.

- **Strategic Impact:** The selection of Vircle aligns with the operational push to modernize cashless systems and parent payments across campuses efficiently, directly tying into [[ESG]] digital initiatives and [[School Management System Integration (SMSI)]] architectures.
