# Sri KL
**Jurisdiction:** Malaysia
**Corporate Entity:** Othman Merican Educational Development Berhad

## Financial & Operational Baselines

### 2010
- **Revenue:** RM 22,856,556 (RM 22.5M from school fees).
- **Profit:** RM 471,523.
- **Strategic Notes:** Holding company was incorporated in 2008 and commenced primary operations in 2010. Committed to significant rental agreements for its campus premises.

### 2011
- **Revenue:** RM 30,302,017 (RM 28.7M from school fees).
- **Profit:** RM 5,353,778.
- **Enrolment:** 263 documented employees.
- **Strategic Notes:** Operates firmly as a non-profit, enjoying tax-exempt status under the Income Tax (Exemption) (No. 5) Order 2008.

### 2013
- **Revenue:** RM 36,126,300 (RM 33.4M from school fees).
- **Profit:** RM 7,766,030.
- **Strategic Notes:** Highly stable domestic institution. The school locked in a massive future rental commitment of RM 103.1M. This profile validates aggressive growth from domestic wealth segments, aligning with the pressure vectors discussed in [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]].
