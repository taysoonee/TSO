# Tanglin Trust School

Tanglin Trust School Limited is a leading premium international school in Singapore, operating as a non-profit company limited by guarantee and a registered charity. It primarily follows a British-based curriculum.

## Financial Performance &amp; Strategy
The school demonstrates strong financial health and a strategy of reinvesting surpluses into campus infrastructure, ensuring it maintains its premium facilities.

### FY2017 (Year ended 31 August 2017)
- **Revenue:** S\$92,478,599
- **Profit (Surplus):** S\$6,314,692
- **Strategic Note:** Revenue is comprised almost entirely of school and enrolment fees. The school recorded S\$1.1 million in 'Placement right income' for priority access, highlighting strong market demand.

### FY2016 (Year ended 31 August 2016)
- **Revenue:** S\$91,654,568
- **Profit (Surplus):** S\$9,545,519
- **Strategic Note:** The school reported robust growth and undertook over S\$42 million in capital expenditure, demonstrating significant investment in its facilities. The large balance of 'School fees billed in advance' (S\$53.8M) indicates very strong forward demand.
