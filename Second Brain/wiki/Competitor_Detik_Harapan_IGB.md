# Competitor Profile: Detik Harapan (IGB Group)

## Operational Status & Financial Health
Detik Harapan Sdn. Bhd., operating primarily in the Sungai Buloh catchment and functioning under IGB Corporation Berhad, demonstrates a high-burn startup trajectory. The entity generated RM 29.9 million in revenue by 2019 (a scale-up from RM 21.1 million in 2017), purely driven by school fees. However, it operates with significant financial deficits, recording a loss of RM 11.9 million in 2019 following a RM 23.9 million loss in 2017. 

The company carries substantial net current liabilities (exceeding RM 195 million as of 2017), maintaining solvency strictly through the continued financial support of its immediate holding company, IGB Corporation Berhad. This high-capital tolerance heavily intersects with [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]], demonstrating the aggressive absorption of operational losses by corporate-backed real estate developers to capture local market share and anchor their developments.

## Strategic Intersections
*   **Catchment Capability:** Sungai Buloh area expansion mapped to [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]].
*   **Corporate Subsidisation:** Sustained by IGB Corporation Berhad despite technical insolvency indicators at the operational level; requires monitoring via [[Competitor Analysis]].
