# Peninsula Education (Setia Alam) Sdn. Bhd.

Peninsula Education (Setia Alam) Sdn. Bhd. is a competitor in the Setia Alam corridor, a key growth area monitored under [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]].

## Financial Performance

### FY2019
- **Revenue:** RM 9,141,638
- **Profit/(Loss):** RM (18,203,914)
- **Strategic Note:** The company recorded a significant net loss and faced 'going concern' issues, with current liabilities exceeding assets by RM 81.8 million. Its continued operation relied on financial support from directors and shareholders. This financial fragility represents a significant market vulnerability. The company anticipated leveraging COVID-19 relief measures, such as loan deferments, to ease cash flow pressures.
