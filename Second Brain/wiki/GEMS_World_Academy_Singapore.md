# GEMS World Academy (Singapore)

GEMS World Academy (Singapore) Pte. Ltd., now rebranded as [[XCL World Academy]], is a key competitor in the Singapore premium international education market. It operates as part of the global GEMS Education network.

## Financial Performance

### FY2016 (Year ended 31 August 2016)
- **Revenue:** S\$10,781,246
- **Profit/(Loss):** S\$(25,311,530)
- **Strategic Note:** The company incurred a significant net loss and its liabilities exceeded assets by S\$34.6 million. The financial statements were prepared on a 'going concern' basis, heavily reliant on financial support from its immediate holding company, GEMS Education (East Asia) Pte. Ltd. This indicates severe financial distress and dependency on its parent group, representing a significant operational vulnerability. The company also incurred substantial marketing expenses of S\$1.2 million.

## Fee Structure &amp; Strategy
GEMS positions itself in the premium tier, offering sibling discounts and scholarships to attract and retain students.

- **2020-2021:** Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$12,010 (Nursery Half Day) to S\$40,640 (IB DP). The school offered sibling discounts (10% for 1st/2nd, 25% for 3rd+) and scholarships. It also provided fee credits/waivers for transfers from other GEMS schools.
[ARCHIVE: GEMS - 2020.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/GEMS World Academy (Singapore)/2020]

- **2019-2020:** Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$12,010 to S\$40,640.
[ARCHIVE: GEMS - 2019.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/GEMS World Academy (Singapore)/2019]

- **2017-2018:** Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$12,600 to S\$39,113. The school announced a 5% increase in Registration and Admission fees for the following year.
[ARCHIVE: GEMS - 2017.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/GEMS World Academy (Singapore)/2017-2018]

- **2015-2016:** Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$9,000 to S\$34,250.
[ARCHIVE: GEMS - 2015.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/Tier-1 Schools/GEMS World Academy (Singapore)/2015]

- **2014-2015:** As an introductory offer, the school provided a 20% "first year discount" on tuition fees, an aggressive market entry strategy. Annual fees after discount ranged from S\$24,800 to S\$36,000.
