# Sri KDU (KDU Smart School)

## Overview
A dominant volume player in the mid-tier (Tier-2) private/international school market in Malaysia.

## Financial & Operational Efficiency
- **Volume & Margins (2014):** Established the benchmark for Tier-2 profitability with a massive student base of 3,059 and RM 72.9m in revenue.
- **Cost Discipline:** Achieved an exceptional 43.8% PBT margin (RM 31.9m) by maintaining an extremely lean staff-cost-to-revenue ratio of just 37.4%. This is significantly leaner than Tier-1 schools, demonstrating the financial viability of high-volume, mid-priced education.
- **Competitive Landscape:** Competes directly for the M40/T20 domestic demographic against [[Taylor's International School]] and [[Sri Kuala Lumpur International School]]. 

## Strategic Impact
Validates the resilience of the mid-tier domestic market, particularly as economic pressures push families toward value-optimized education (see [[T20 Inflation Squeeze]]).
