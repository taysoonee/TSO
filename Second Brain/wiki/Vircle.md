# Vircle\n\nVircle (operated by Debit Circles Sdn. Bhd.) is the preferred e-wallet provider for Taylor's Schools, facilitating cashless campus operations.\n\n## Governance & Compliance\n- **Principal License Holder:** Fass Payment Sdn. Bhd. (Fasspay).\n- **Regulatory Status:** E-wallet functionality regulated by Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM) under the Financial Services Act 2013.\n- **Merchant Framework:** Operates under the 'Fasspay White Label Wallet Terms and Conditions (Merchants)'.\n\n## Operational Capabilities\n- **Interfaces:** Merchant App, Software, static QR codes, and Contactless Payment cards.\n- **Transaction Logic:** Supports in-app digital payments and real-time settlement reporting.\n- **Dispute Resolution:** 14-day investigation window for merchants; Charge Back mechanism managed via Fasspay/Vircle.\n\n## Strategic Integration\n- **Selection Logic:** Scored 87% in group evaluation due to zero maintenance costs and 40-day deployment speed.\n- **Financial Hedge:** Supports [[The Living Ledger]] Thesis 2 (Domestic Inflation Squeeze) by improving operational efficiency and reducing cash handling overheads.\n- **SMSI Link:** Interfaces with [[School Management System Integration (SMSI)]] for external financial reporting and student pocket money management.\n\n--- \nSource: [[Fasspay White Label Wallet Terms and Conditions (Merchants) 20190718 - Vircle.pdf]]
