# International Community School (ICS)
**Jurisdiction:** Singapore

## Financial & Operational Baselines

### 2017–2018
- **Revenue:** S$ 11,780,650 (includes S$ 11.3M from tuition).
- **Profit:** S$ 4,141,750 operating position against total income.
- **Enrolment:** 481 students.
- **Strategic Notes:** Focus on long-term expansion through the 'ICS Legacy Fund', targeting S$ 3M over three years. Actively pursued relocation to a new facility. Maintains financial reserves equivalent to roughly six months of operating expenses. Full EduTrust certification secured.
