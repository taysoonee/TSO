# EtonHouse International School

EtonHouse International School Pte Ltd is a competitor in the Singapore private school market, part of the wider EtonHouse group.

## Financial Performance

### FY2016 (Year ended 31 December 2016)
- **Revenue:** S\$9,645,205
- **Profit:** S\$1,082,318
- **Strategic Note:** The company demonstrated healthy profitability. It has a significant concentration of credit risk with balances due from its holding company and related entities, highlighting strong inter-group financial flows.
