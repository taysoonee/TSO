# Market Intelligence

Market Intelligence at Taylor's Schools is a strategic function aimed at enabling "business intimacy to external environment" and driving business planning through actionable data [[Tay's Market Intelligence Role and Responsibility.pdf]].

## Research Domains

### Macro Research
Continuous tracking of external factors:
- Latest education trends.
- Growth sectors within the education industry.
- Migration trends (specifically for Asian Expatriates).

### Actionable Research
Directly informs tactical decisions:
- **Pricing Reviews:** Periodic research to align fees with market value.
- **Student Attrition Analysis:** Understanding why students leave (non-graduation) to improve retention.
- **Fee Increase Research:** Data support for adjusting tuition fees.
- **Surveys:** Admission and marketing surveys conducted during Open Days.

## Performance Tracking
- **Admission Funnel:** Monitoring the journey from inquiry to enrollment.
- **Marketing Effectiveness:** ROI analysis of campaigns.
- **Competitor Analytics:** Integration of competitors' financial data into visualization tools (e.g., DataStudio).

## Key Projects
- **M&A Support:** Providing data analytics for potential mergers and acquisitions.
- **Sustainability:** Solar Panels installation projects.
- **ESG:** Environmental, Social, and Governance reporting and initiatives.

## Methods & Tools
- **Data Visualization:** Integration of data into **DataStudio** (Looker Studio) for executive reporting.
- **Competitor Financial Tracking:** Acquisition of **SSM Reports** (Suruhanjaya Syarikat Malaysia) to benchmark competitor revenue, profitability, and growth [[Request for Payment - Apr 2026.pdf]].
- **Normalization:** Utilization of [[Grade Equivalency]] tables to align data across international curricula.

## Update (May 12, 2026): SMSI Marketing Analytics & Logic
The [[School Management System Integration (SMSI)]] incorporates specific logic for tracking marketing effectiveness and financial aid:

### Cost of Acquisition (COA) Tracking
TM1 tracks COA per new enrolment across three categories:
1. **Total Marketing COA:** `All Marketing Cost / Enrolment by All Marketing Channels`.
2. **A&P COA:** `Advertisement & Promotion Cost / Enrolment (Excluding Agency Channel)`.
3. **Agent Fee COA:** `Agents Commission Fees / Enrolment by Agency Channel`.

### Scholarship & Discount Capture Logic
For the TM1 system to accurately capture percentage values in automated reports, a mandatory naming convention is required for all entries in the SMS:
- **Format:** `[Type]-X%` (e.g., `Academic Scholarship-100%`, `Loyalty Discount-10%`).
- **Failure to Comply:** If the description does not end with '-X%', the system treats the amount as a fixed sum rather than a percentage, potentially distorting [[Market Intelligence]] ROI calculations.

Source: [[Taylors_SMSI _User Training Manual_v1.2.pdf]]
