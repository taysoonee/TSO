# HELP Education Services Sdn. Bhd.

HELP Education Services Sdn. Bhd. is a competitor in the Malaysian private education market, with activities from pre-school to pre-university levels. It operates as part of the larger HELP International Corporation Berhad group.

## Financial Performance &amp; Strategy

### FY2018
- **Revenue:** RM 51,297,948
- **Profit:** RM 9,021,910
- **Strategic Note:** A significant strategic development was the entry into a joint venture with Ibraco Berhad to establish a new private school in Kuching, Sarawak. This expansion into East Malaysia is a key move related to [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]]. The company's continued operation relied on financial support from its parent company.

### FY2017
- **Revenue:** RM 43,614,453
- **Profit:** RM 6,098,536
- **Strategic Note:** The company maintained a 30% ownership interest in Crescendo International School Sdn. Bhd., indicating a strategy of strategic partnerships. Its reliance on financial support from its holding company could be a vulnerability in a market sensitive to [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]].

### FY2016 (14-month period)
- **Revenue:** RM 41,673,283
- **Profit:** RM 17,294,303
- **Strategic Note:** Acquired a 30% shareholding in Crescendo International School Sdn. Bhd. for RM 7.5 million, confirming its expansion and partnership strategy.
