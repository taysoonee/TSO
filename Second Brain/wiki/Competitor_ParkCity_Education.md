# Competitor Profile: ParkCity Education

## Operational Status & Financial Health
Formerly known as ParkCity-Brighton Education, ParkCity Education Sdn. Bhd. is a profoundly successful tier-one institution backed by Perdana ParkCity Sdn. Bhd. In 2019, the institution recorded overall revenues of RM 66.2 million (with direct tuition fees accounting for RM 61.3 million), driving a strong net profit of RM 14.6 million.
