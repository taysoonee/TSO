
## Pricing & Operational Baseline (2019-20)
- **2019-20 Pricing:** For the 2019-20 academic year, new High School enrolments (Lane 2-4: Non-US passport holders) tuition and facility fee total was **SGD 54,841**. Registration fee was **SGD 9,630**.
- **Application Fee:** **SGD 2,500**.
- **Other Costs:** Interim Semester averaged **SGD 3,025**.
