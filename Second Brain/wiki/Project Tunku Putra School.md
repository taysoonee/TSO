# Project Tunku Putra School

## Executive Summary
This project involved a feasibility study and potential acquisition of **Tunku Putra School (TPS)** in Kuching, Sarawak, by Taylor's Education Sdn Bhd (TEG). After a detailed evaluation in late 2014 and early 2015, Taylor's decided **not to pursue** the acquisition as of June 5, 2015. The analysis focused on rebranding TPS to the **Taylor's International School (TIS) model** to improve financial performance and enrolment.

## Key Metrics (as of 2014/2015)
- **Status:** Declined (as of 5 June 2015).
- **Owner:** Cahya Mata Sarawak Berhad (CMS).
- **Location:** Jalan Stadium, Petra Jaya, 93050 Kuching, Sarawak.
- **Financials:**
    - **Nett Profit/Loss (2014):** (RM 5.23 Million) loss.
    - **Nett Profit/Loss (2015 Est):** (RM 5.26 Million) loss.
    - **Cost per Student (2014):** RM 26,000.
    - **Average Fee per Student (2014):** RM 17,000.
- **Enrolment:**
    - **Total Students (2014):** 587 students.
    - **New Students (2014):** 143 students.
    - **Expat-Local Ratio:** 1:3 (2014).

## Strategic Analysis & Proposed Rebranding
The proposal involved transitioning TPS to the **TIS Model**:
- **Capex Investment:** Estimated RM 10 million to RM 15 million for campus upgrades (classrooms, library, science labs, sports facilities, ICT infrastructure).
- **Target Financials (2017):**
    - **Target Cost per Student:** RM 24,000.
    - **Target Average Fee:** RM 30,000.
    - **Target Enrolment:** 1,200 students.

## Competitor Benchmarking (2014)
- **[[Tenby International School Miri]]:** Higher fees (e.g., Year 1 at ~RM 20k/year, Year 11 at ~RM 32k/year).
- [[Lodge Group of Schools]] (Kuching): Lower fees (e.g., Year 10-11 International at ~RM 10.8k/year).
- [[St. Joseph's Private School]] (SJPS): Mentioned as a major private school competitor in Kuching.
