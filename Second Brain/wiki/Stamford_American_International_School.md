# Stamford American International School (SAIS)

Stamford American International School (SAIS) Pte. Ltd. is a major competitor in the Singapore premium international education market, operating as part of the Cognita group.

## Strategic Positioning
SAIS positions itself as a premium provider offering IB and US curricula. A key part of its strategy is a strong focus on early years acquisition and retention through its 'Early Learning Village' and Infant Care Programme (from 2 months old). This creates a long-term student pipeline, a crucial strategy in a competitive market.

## Financial Performance

### FY2017 (Year ended 31 August 2017)
- **Revenue:** S\$109,961,073
- **Profit:** S\$9,271,182
- **Strategic Note:** The company reported net current liabilities of S\$159.5 million, but operated as a 'going concern' due to financial support from its parent, Cognita. This financial dependency is a key structural point.
