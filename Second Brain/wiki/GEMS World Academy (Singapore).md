# GEMS World Academy (Singapore)

## Pricing & Fees
### 2019-2020 Academic Year
- **Source:** [[GEMS - 2019.pdf]]
- **Tuition Fees (Annual):**
  - **Early Years:** Nursery Full Day at SGD 21,570 (MYR ~66.6k); Pre-K Full Day at SGD 23,790 (MYR ~73.5k); K1-K2 Full Day ranging from SGD 27,080 to SGD 33,200 (MYR ~83.6k - 102.5k).
  - **Primary (G1-G5):** SGD 33,200 - SGD 34,070 (MYR ~102.5k - 105.2k).
  - **Secondary (G6-G10):** SGD 39,270 (MYR ~121.3k).
  - **IB CP/DP (G11-G12):** SGD 40,277 - SGD 40,640 (MYR ~124.4k - 125.5k).
- **Additional Fees:** EAL Level 1 at SGD 8,400 (MYR ~25.9k). Fees are inclusive of 7% GST.
- **Strategic Impact:** The ultra-premium cost of secondary/IB education in Singapore directly supports our [[Singapore K-12 Market]] value proposition and [[The Living Ledger]]'s [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate & FDI Inflow Vector]]. Taylor's Schools offer significant value to Singapore-based expatriate or regional families when comparing boarding and tuition combinations.


### 2015-2016 Academic Year
- **Source:** [[GEMS - 2015.pdf]]
- **Tuition Fees (Annual):**
  - **Early Years:** Pre-K/K1 Half Day SGD 9,000 (MYR ~31.5k); K1 Full Day SGD 21,800 (MYR ~76.3k); K2 Full Day SGD 27,400 (MYR ~95.9k).
  - **Primary (G1-G5):** SGD 27,400 - SGD 28,200 (MYR ~95.9k - 98.7k).
  - **Secondary (G6-G11):** SGD 33,000 - SGD 34,250 (MYR ~115.5k - 119.9k).
- **Mandatory Additional Fees:** Registration SGD 1,500 (MYR ~5.3k); Admission SGD 4,000 (MYR ~14.0k); Annual Innovation Fee SGD 1,000 (MYR ~3.5k); Annual Facilities Fee SGD 2,000 (MYR ~7.0k). All fees inclusive of 7% GST.
- **Strategic Impact:** Establishes the historical baseline (2015) for premium tier pricing in the Singapore market, highlighting the compounding of tuition fees over time. The structural separation of facilities and innovation fees reveals alternative revenue generation strategies for premium campuses. Supports tracking for [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate & FDI Inflow Vector]] and price elasticity mapping in the [[Singapore K-12 Market]].
