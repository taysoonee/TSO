# Sirius International (M) Sdn. Bhd.

Sirius International (M) Sdn. Bhd. is a competitor providing international school education in the Malaysian market.

## Financial Performance

### FY2017
- **Revenue (Group):** RM 32,507,195
- **Profit/(Loss) (Group):** RM (3,664,921)
- **Strategic Note:** The Group reported a significant net loss, suggesting financial challenges at the group or subsidiary level, even as the company itself reported a profit.

### FY2016
- **Revenue (Group):** RM 30,545,788
- **Profit/(Loss) (Group):** RM (970,316)
- **Strategic Note:** The Group shifted from a profit in 2015 to a loss in 2016, despite a 37% increase in revenue. This points to rising operational costs or investments impacting profitability, a trend relevant to the pressures described in [[Thesis 2: Domestic M40/T20 Inflation Squeeze]]. Auditors issued a 'going concern' warning that appeared to contradict the reported positive performance of the company (not the group), warranting closer investigation of its overall financial health.
