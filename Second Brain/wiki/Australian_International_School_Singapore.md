# Australian International School (Singapore)

Australian International School (AIS) Pte. Ltd. is a key competitor in the Singapore international school market, part of the global Cognita group. It provides education for expatriate and regional students, following the New South Wales curriculum.

## Financial Performance

### FY2017 (Year ended 31 August 2017)
- **Revenue:** S\$87,723,246
- **Profit:** S\$13,681,591
- **Strategic Note:** The company reported net current liabilities of S\$275 million but operated as a 'going concern' due to financial support from its parent company, Cognita. It was undertaking a significant campus expansion project at Lorong Chuan.

## Fee Structure &amp; Strategy
AIS offers aggressive multi-child discounts to attract and retain large families, a key competitive strategy.

- **2024:** Annual tuition fees for Secondary (Y10-12) were S\$50,196. The school offered substantial sibling discounts, including a 100% waiver on enrolment and facility fees for the 4th child onwards.
[ARCHIVE: AIS - 2024.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/Australian International School/2024]

- **2023:** Annual tuition for Secondary (Y10-12) was S\$47,140.
