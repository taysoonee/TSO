# Dulwich College (Singapore)

Dulwich College (Singapore) Pte. Ltd. is a premium Tier-1 competitor in the Singapore international school market, part of the Dulwich College Management International Limited (DCMI) group.

## Financial Performance

### FY2017 (Year ended 31 July 2017)
- **Revenue:** S\$54,995,745
- **Profit/(Loss):** S\$(3,190,434)
- **Strategic Note:** Despite a 33% increase in revenue, the company reported a net loss of S\$3.19 million. Its operation as a 'going concern' was dependent on financial support from its parent company, DCMI, due to current liabilities exceeding assets by S\$20.6 million. This indicates significant financial vulnerability and reliance on its parent group, a crucial piece of competitor intelligence. Total borrowings stood at a substantial S\$160.2 million.

### FY2016 (Year ended 31 July 2016)
- **Revenue:** S\$41,259,293
- **Profit:** S\$1,109,546
- **Strategic Note:** The company's 'going concern' status was also dependent on parent company support in this period. It had significant capital expenditure commitments and substantial borrowings (S\$153 million) related to campus construction, indicating a period of heavy investment.

## Fee Structure &amp; Strategy
Dulwich maintains a top-tier fee structure, including boarding options, positioning it firmly in the premium segment. This aligns with [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate &amp; FDI Inflow Vector]] and [[Thesis 3: Boarding as a Premium Utility]].

- **2023-2024:** Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$18,650 (Toddler Half Day) to S\$51,730 (Years 12-13). Annual boarding fees were S\$37,800. The school increased its Application Fee and Capital Levy from 1 August 2023 and noted the impending GST increase to 9%.
[ARCHIVE: DCS - 2023.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/Dulwich College (Singapore)/2023]

- **2021-2022:** Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$17,200 to S\$47,730. A 5% sibling discount for three or more children was offered.
[ARCHIVE: DCS - 2021.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/Dulwich College Singapore/2021-2022]

- **2020-2021:** The school offered a one-time 15% "loyalty discount" on Term 3 2019/20 fees for eligible students, credited to Term 1 2020/21. This was a clear retention strategy, likely in response to COVID-19 uncertainty.
[ARCHIVE: DCS - 2020.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/Dulwich College Singapore/2020]

- **2018-2019:** Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$16,400 (Toddler Half Day) to S\$45,500 (Year 12).
