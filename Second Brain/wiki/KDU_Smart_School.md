# KDU Smart School Sdn. Bhd.

## Paramount Group Optimisation
Functioning as the dedicated K-12 educational subsidiary of Paramount Corporation Berhad, KDU demonstrates exceptional operational efficiency. From 2011 to 2012, revenue shifted from RM 38.8 million up to RM 49.2 million, propelling net yields from RM 11.2 million to an impressive RM 14.5 million.

## Balance Sheet Restructuring
During this high-growth phase, Paramount intervened to eliminate systemic capital risk. By issuing RM 22.8 million in Non-cumulative Redeemable Convertible Preference Shares (NCRCPS), KDU aggressively slashed its debt-to-equity vulnerability from 40% down to 21% inside a single financial year, rendering them highly insulated against subsequent economic cycles.
