# Raffles American School (RAS)

## Overview
Raffles American School (RAS) provides an American-style education in [[Iskandar Malaysia]]. It is a subsidiary of Raffles Education Corporation (Singapore) and was founded in partnership with International School Services (ISS).

## Campus & Expansion
- **Anjung Campus (2012):** Opened in August 2012 at Anjung Neighborhood Center, Nusajaya, for Grades 1-8.
- **Flagship Campus (2014):** A 46-acre campus in Iskandar was completed in August 2014, accommodating Pre-K to Grade 12.

## Strategic Context
The entry of RAS into the [[Johor Market]] in 2012 coincided with the [[The Johor Factor|Iskandar Education Hub]] push, competing directly with [[Marlborough College Malaysia]] and later [[Taylor's International School]] brands for premium and expat segments.
