# Overseas Family School (OFS)

Overseas Family School (OFS) is a major competitor in the Singapore international school market, known for its open-entry admission policy and year-round admissions.

## Strategic Positioning
OFS's key differentiator is its accessibility. It has no entrance tests and does not require English proficiency for admission, which broadens its potential market significantly among the expatriate community. Pro-rated fees for year-round admissions cater directly to the mobile nature of families targeted by [[Thesis 1: The Expatriate &amp; FDI Inflow Vector]].

## Fee Structure Analysis
OFS maintains a premium fee structure, with noticeable price adjustments and a clear reflection of GST changes.

- **2023-2024:** Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$17,100 (Pre-K1 Half Day) to S\$44,400 (Senior High). Fees included 8% GST.
[ARCHIVE: OFS - 2023.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/Overseas Family School/2023]

- **2022-2023:** A noticeable fee increase occurred between Semester 1 and Semester 2, coinciding with the GST increase to 8%. Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$16,550 to S\$42,700.
[ARCHIVE: OFS - 2022.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/Overseas Family School/2022]

- **2021-2022:** Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$16,000 to S\$41,000, with 7% GST.
[ARCHIVE: OFS - 2021.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/Overseas Family School/2021-2022]

- **2020-2021:** Annual tuition fees ranged from S\$16,000 to S\$41,000.
