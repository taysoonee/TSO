# One World International School (OWIS)

One World International School (OWIS) is a competitor in the Singapore market, positioning itself as providing 'moderately priced' world-class education. It is part of the Global Schools Foundation.

## Strategic Positioning &amp; Expansion
OWIS follows a value-for-money strategy to capture a segment of the market seeking quality international education without the highest premium price tag. The school is actively expanding its footprint in Singapore.

- **Expansion:** The school operates campuses at Nanyang and Suntec, with a new 'Digital Campus' in Punggol scheduled to open in 2023. This expansion is a significant market event, relevant to [[Thesis 4: Greenfield/Brownfield Saturation Limits]].
- **Promotions:** To drive enrolment for the new campus, OWIS offered a S\$1,500 'Founding Family Discount' for applications completed by April 2023.

## Fee Structure Analysis
- **2023-2024 (Digital Campus):** Total annual fees (Tuition + Building) were S\$25,882 (EC-G6) and S\$28,857 (G7-8), with the S\$1,500 discount available.
[ARCHIVE: OWIS Digital - 2023.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/One World International School/2023]

- **2022-2023:** Annual fees ranged from S\$19,659 (EC 1-2) to S\$23,001 (G1-6), exclusive of GST.
[ARCHIVE: OWIS Suntec - 2023.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/One World International School/2023]

- **2020-2021:** The school implemented a 'Solidarity Scheme' that froze fees at 2019-20 levels in response to market conditions (likely COVID-19). Annual tuition was S\$17,793 (G1-6) and S\$20,457 (G7-12), exclusive of GST.
[ARCHIVE: OWIS - 2020.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/One World International School/2020]

- **2019-2020:** Annual fees ranged from S\$17,514 (EC) to S\$20,136 (G7-10), exclusive of GST.
[ARCHIVE: OWIS - 2019.pdf -&gt; 78. Financial &amp; Audited Reports/2 Singapore Schools/One World International School/2019]

- **2016-2017:** Annual fees ranged from S\$17,187 (EC Short Day) to S\$28,055 (G6-9), inclusive of GST.
