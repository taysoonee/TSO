## 📈 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: THE CONVERSION PARADOX
Analysis of the HubSpot live pipeline data as of **June 11, 2026**, reveals a striking **Conversion Paradox**: TISKL’s top-of-funnel marketing engine is performing at historic levels, yet the school is facing a contraction in actual enrolments.

*   **Top-of-Funnel Boom:** YTD (Jan-May) **Enquiries are up +25.2%** (1,229 vs. 982) and **Campus Visits/Tours are up +66.9%** (776 vs. 465).
*   **Bottom-of-Funnel Leakage:** Despite the influx of visitors, YTD **Applications have dropped by -11.7%** (233 vs. 264) and **Acceptances have fallen by -6.2%** (120 vs. 128).
*   **The Visit-to-Application Bottleneck:** The core conversion rate from **Visit to Application has collapsed from 56.8% in 2025 to 30.0% in 2026**. 

TISKL has a massive backlog of **463 toured leads sitting inactive** in the pipeline. Converting just 20% of this toured backlog will secure the target of **447 new enrolments** for 2026, beating the budget of 431. This report isolates the drop-off points by nationality, owner performance, and academic term, and provides an action-packed playbook to convert this backlog immediately.

## 🛠️ THE ADMISSIONS TEAM ACTION PLAYBOOK (Q2/Q3 2026)

### Action 1: Target the "Visited" Backlog (Immediate Campaign)
*   **The Opportunity:** There are **463 toured leads** sitting inactive in the Visited stage for T3 2026 (258) and T1 2027 (205).
*   **Tactical Execution:**
    1.  Divide the backlog among the admissions team by nationality and year group.
    2.  Launch a **"Fast-Track Assessment" campaign** for the June-July school holiday window. Offer a 50% waiver on assessment fees for any family who toured in the last 90 days and registers for an assessment within the next 14 days.
    3.  Focus heavily on **Year 5 for T3 2026** (78 leads) and **Year 1/Nursery for T1 2027** (159 leads).

### Action 2: Expatriate Conversion and Visa Support
*   **The Opportunity:** Address the high leakage in Chinese (25.9% conversion) and Korean (9.8% conversion) pipelines.
*   **Tactical Execution:**
    1.  Create a dedicated **"KL Visa Concierge Service"** within the admissions team. Since KL visa guidelines are highly restrictive (RM25,000 income requirement, 2 applications/day cap), provide pre-vetted immigration agent pathways to parents during their campus tour.
    2.  Develop Korean-specific and Chinese-specific tour follow-up emails, highlighting English-as-an-Additional-Language (EAL) success stories (specifically mentioning top performers like Natalie Chan and Efron Goh who achieved possible "Top in the World" in IGCSE Maths).

### Action 3: Turnaround Response Times to Under 48 Hours
*   **The Opportunity:** Reduce the average assessment turnaround from 72-109 hours to under 48 hours to minimize competitive defection.
*   **Tactical Execution:**
    1.  Implement automated HubSpot alerts for Deal Owners when a deal moves to "Ready for Assessment".
    2.  Cross-train staff to ensure that during peak periods, assessments are graded and offered within 24 hours of completion.

### Action 4: Counter the Barbell Defection Trend (Leverage ESG & Academic Pride)
*   **The Opportunity:** Under the [[T20 Inflation Squeeze]], TISKL is losing price-sensitive families laterally/downwards (to Wesley Methodist, Stella Maris, St. John) and wealthier families upwards (to GIS, ISKL).
*   **Tactical Execution:**
    1.  **Defend Against Downward Defection:** Highlight TISKL's premium features and value-add. Emphasize that TISKL has locked in energy costs via the **190.75 kWp Solar PV System** investment (protecting families from tuition fee hikes driven by electricity tariff inflation). Highlight our status as a **Quantum Learning Distinguished School (QLDS)**.
    2.  **Capture Upward Defectors:** Target families looking to downgrade from Tier-1 premium schools (like GIS) by positioning TISKL as the high-ROI alternative that achieves elite academic results (55.6% A\*-A in IGCSE 2024) at a fraction of the cost.
