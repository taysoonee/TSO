Marketing teams must ensure our communications clearly articulate our own technological and curriculum modernisations to remain competitive against entrenched premium brands.

Marketing must leverage our recent group-wide solar investments to counter this established operational narrative and underscore our ESG commitments to cost-conscious and environmentally aware parent segments.
